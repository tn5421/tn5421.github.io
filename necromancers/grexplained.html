Guild Ranks Explained

Everyone starts out with the Undead rank. You can change this rank by participating in guild dungeons and collect Guild Dungeon Points, (GDP).

Below is the current ranking system and may be subject to change without notice:


Undead Standard rank given upon joining the guild
Skeleton Achieved at 1k GDP
Zombie Achieved at 5k GDP
Ghoul Achieved at 10k GDP
Mummy Achieved at 25k GDP
Vampire Achieved at 50k GDP
Lich Achieved at 100k GDP: Starting with this rank you are give map permissions
Wight Achieved at 200k GDP: Starting with this rank you also have festival permissions
Revenant Achieved at 300k GDP
Poltergeist Achieved at 400k GDP
Draugr Achieved at 500k GDP

Specialty ranks: These ranks are special and come with different guild permissions:

NecroMaster Leader of the guild
Grave Digger In charge of the guild maps
Dead Drunk Guild recruiter
Crypt Keeper Able to open and close guild maps
Asmodeus In charge of the guild lotteries
Dark Summoner In charge of the guild boss
Master of Tomes In charge of the guild database

In addition to these ranks you can also add a prefix title by donating more experience toward the guild. Please note: This extra experience is set with the title and cannot be lowered in your personal settings

Elite Give 25% experience
Overlord Give 50% experience
Demigod Give 75% Experience
