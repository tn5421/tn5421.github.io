Guild Maps



Guild maps are like personal maps, with the following exceptions:

All gold/plat/jade/gems looted from rooms go to the guild

All experience from the mobs go to the guild

All Dungeon Points, (DP), go to the guild

Any drops you find in the guild map go to you

Participation in guild maps is non mandatory, but we do offer several incentives to those who partcipate:

Anyone who gains a minimum of 1,000 Guild Dungeon Points, (GDP), in a month is automatically entered into the Monthly Lotto

We offer a monthly reward system based on the amount of GDP you collect. You can win each prize once a month, no limit on how high you can go.

1,000 GDP ......... 5 plat

2,500 GDP ......... 5 plat

5,000 GDP ....... 10 plat

10,000 GDP...... 10 plat

15,000 GDP...... 15 plat

20,000 GDP...... 20 plat

25,000 GDP...... 25 plat

30,000 GDP..... 30 plat

The pattern of an extra 5 plat per 5k GDP continues indefinitely
