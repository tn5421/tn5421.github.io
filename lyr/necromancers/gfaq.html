Guild FAQ

  1. What are the guild requirements

    2. Activity is the main requirement. Activity is monitored at least once a week and you may be removed if your activity is too low based on your level. There is also a minimum of 20% guild tax

    If you need to be away from the game for a few days, please let BloodGood know or you may be kicked without notice

    All new members are under a two,(2), week probation period to ensure you are a good fit with our guild

  2. Guild Conduct

    3. Harassing guild members is not permitted. If you are having an issue with a guild member you can always use the /ignore to ignore them. If you are still have issues after ignoring then, then contact BloodGood

    Please use some common sense when in guild chat. We have a variety of member with different backgrounds and beliefs, please take this into consideration

    If an officer asks to you to end a topic of discussion, please do so. If you don't agree with the officer, please end the topic anyways and contact BloodGood

  3. Guild and Main chat rules

    4. The use of racial or homophobic slurs are absolutely forbidden. First offense will result in a warning from an officer. The second offense will result in termination from the guild.

  4. I keep seeing something like this "Guild Statue Drops: E J"

    5. Those are personal drops you get from the Guild Statues. Each letter stands for a gem and the "J" is for jade

  5. What are guild ranks

    6. Guild ranks are explained here

  6. What time is the guild boss

    7. Boss times are generally between 1:15 - 2:00 server time unless otherwise noted

  7. I have a guild related question, who should I ask

    8. BloodGood: Guild leader, chief decision maker and supplier of jewelry

    Alice: In charge of the weekly and monthly lottery

    Karubo: Database designer
