All 11 of these tasks must be done with a Tiny Bee in your inventory and can only be done with their particular Seal Vertice is active. Entries marked with ,[-] are the currently active selections.

  • [X] Have a Lucha training level of 108 or higher, then fight El Diablo Supreme.
  • [X] Nuke the Gas Station.
  • [X] Say “Now I Stand Alone” on Hidden Forbidden Holy Ground.
  • [X] Use 11 BillyCon perks on the same day.
  • [X] Summon 11 times on the same day.
  • [X] Win a Glowslinging duel in Every Turf on the same day.
  • [X] Consume 1 of each essence in a single day.
  • [X] See yourself as a Winner on the First Loser board
  • [X] Turn in SSSSAAAAFFFFEEEE Emblems in order
  • [X] See your uncorrupted other half in the Fields
