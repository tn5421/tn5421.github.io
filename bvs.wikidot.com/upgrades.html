Upgrades

Through Upgrades you improve your Village. Some of them are useful for Village development while others provide a bonus for your Villagers.

Kaiju (Kaijukage) get 50% off costs!
Those with Level 1 / Level 2 / Level 3 of Shady Dealings will get discount on the upgrades

  • Primary Resources
  • Secondary Resources
  • Advanced Resources
  • Kaiju
  • Special

These are purchased by your village in exchange for Ryo and your village's primary Resource.

Upgrade Bonus
Banker Base Upkeep is halved. Village Contracts may be submitted.
BillyCon Center Allows Billycon Registration
Chunin Training School Chunin get +1 stamina per successful C-and D-Rank Mission
Claw Machines Add a Claw Machine To the Party House.
Collection Squad 3% Base Collection Bonus per collector
Creepy Traps Bingo Ninja that fail Spying/Attacking you

Ninja Attack Squad -1 Difficulty and -1 Success when attacking Villages
Nonja Farm Allow the buying of Nonjas
Overseers +5% Base Collect Bonus for Genin and Chunin
Party House Villagers recieve a base 20% discount at the Party House
RoboGas Station +10000 Daily RoboFighto Ryo (Machine Shop required)
Saboteur School -1 Successes needed when attacking Villages
Science! Facility Allows creation of Science! Upgrades
Sentry Squads 15% better spy detection
Siege Engines -2 Difficulty when attacking Villages
Solidarity +6 Difficulty needed when being spied on by a much larger Village (>5 size)
Spy Towers 2 more successes needed for enemy spies
War Council Your Village may enter the Ninja World War

These upgrades require resources that your village does not produce. To be able to obtain them you must have the War Council Upgrade, join the great Ninja World War, and take them from other villages.

Upgrade Bonus

Tasty Ramen Shop +10 Stamina per day

War Room All ninja get 2 extra arena attacks per day

Verdant Fields Unlocks Flower Wars in Party House
