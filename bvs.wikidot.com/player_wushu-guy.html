Character Description

"Man, a football-playing nin from some gaming portal?"

History

Out of utter boredom, this character was spawn from the depths of emerald hell. No one knew what he was capable of, until he summoned the greatest evil that no one has ever encountered. But a beautiful young warrior banished the evil in this man's heart and she went on to free his soul of wretchedness. Now, he is known as Wushu Guy - a man seeking for the woman who made him what he is right now.

Wushu Guy, known before as Ace - a maniacal thinker with obsessive compulsive disorder, once destroyed four hundred men in a second after he found out that the girl she loved and cherished was abused by these men with their weapon of choice - flirting. Enraged, Ace showed no mercy as delivered destructive blows on his foes. Decapitating them with a 24.15423 cm laceration on their necks with his 194561354.54212 N of force injected at his fingertips. With such a heavy blow, an average man has less than .000451200123 of a second to live upon impact. Ace had nothing after that. Only his physique made to crumble even the toughest glacier found in the arctic.

Ace was faced with a lot of hardships that he viewed as his failures and shortcomings. He lost his family, friends, and loved ones as they were murdered one by one in front of his very eyes. He sought vengeance for all the lives wasted in front of him. He eventually met the greatest Zen Master alive, Wushu Girl. He then fell in love with Wushu Girl and Ace eventually forgotten his past filled with rage and bloodlust as he was rechristened as Wushu Guy. All's well, ends well, right? Unfortunately, a group of zit-faced ninjas abducted Wushu Girl. Determined to retrieve the love of his life, Wushu Guy went to Dotaportal Village to ask the help of the Kage, Kai. With the help of his sempais of Hidan, mikelorus, Khrono, and deelle, Wushu Guy would again fight for his love and his newfound family.
