Get Chunin

▼ Walk-through details
▲ Hide the details
  • Get ready
  • You will need: 100-300 stamina, 20-50 Smoke Bombs, 8 Jutsus, 40 D-rank missions, 6 Allies, try getting
  • Stalkergirl, Quest Stalkergirl (need 1 Medicinal Herbs)
  • Pinky and/or Billy and/or Emosuke (pretty easy, found in multiple missions)
  • Bruce-Jr, D-rank Nin Blindfighting (if you plan sparring)
  • Lil-Shammy, Quest Checkmate (easy to get level 2 later)
  • Meatballs, Quest Very Tragic Story (easy to get level 2 later, useful for levelling Lil-Whitey)
  • Red-Rover, D-Rank Gen Clean the Ninja Dog Pen (put him in your team and get 6 dog treats)
  • Spot, Quest Walk the Dog (easy to get level 2 later, awesome jutsu)
  • while running D & C rank missions, try to get, then level up the allies mentioned here (at least 3 of them)
  • the higher your crank, the faster you are done with this.
  • NOTE: It is advisable to start by teaming up with Flipper + whomever (Proof Reader if you've got him), do the Stalkergirl and any other quest you want, then team up with Stalkergirl and Flipper for that sweet extra stamina & AP.
  • NOTE2: If you have a couple of team changes left over (or have the Mysterious Power Bloodline), it is recommended that you hang out with Flipper (using the Bloodline power, a Stuffed Walrus or a Make Out Mood Enhancer) in order to facilitate Jutsu learning.
  • a. Get level 12 Costs: stamina 0-200
  • b. Quest Chunin Exam Part 1 Costs: stamina 0
  • c. Quest Forest of Death Costs: stamina 50
  • d. Quest Final Fight Costs: stamina 50

Finish Arena content

▼ Walk-through details
▲ Hide the details
  • TIPS and INFO:
  • you should do this as sp.jon, it's way easier.
  • you will need to finish the arena content 3 times, once for each basic bloodline, in order to get your Warrior of ~ item each time.
  • GET READY
  • a. get 1 mil jxp (Jutsu Experience) to buy The Gladiator
  • b. play some tsukiball, have at least 40 tickets at bay to buy the Plastic Ninja Stars
  • c. save some cash for the Party House's Juice section, to buy Doughman Daiquiri (~3600 per 1 fight).
  • d. play arena daily, try to have 3-4 Arena Coupon worth of reputation saved up (~800 rep).
  • FILL THE CONTENT
  • e. Get Doughman
  • - when you hit 8-9 fight wins (preferably 9) pump (in this order) PNS, 1-2 Daiquiri, The Gladiator, Arena Coupons
  • f. Get Warrior of ~
  • calc. your current win/total tickets ratio, example 8 wins / 10 fights = 80%, sum up the MAX of what you can get together from all other items presented at "get ready" and apply that ratio to that MAX. ex: 2 daq +10 Gladiator +2 tsuki +4coupons = 18; 80% of 18 = 14.4 (14 fights win)
  • decide: if your current fights-win + the approximated fights-win from the extra coupons >=15, then start using your extras.
  • in this order: tsukiball, gladiator, daiquiri, coupons

Phase Fighting

▼ Short info
▲ Hide the details

Failing to kill a phase (running out of lives or electing to autofail) will corrupt you.
Killing a phase gets you its normal drop.
You must be r00t rank to drain a phase. Draining a phase destroys that normal drop, gives you the phase's drain drop, and corrupts you.

When corrupted, you cannot fight phases and you lose 30 stamina per day. You'll have to pay a total amount of stamina equal to the "Risk" of the phase you fought in order to clear corruption: this is between 200 stamina and 1750 stamina. Corruption can be paid off over several days.

(See the existing Phases)

▼ Preparation details, short term
▲ Hide the details
  • Before you get started
  • What is a hack?
    • A hack is where hunt down another player in the fields and hack them to gain +1 drain str.
    • Go to the Field: Hunt and Hack Someone to start a hack.
    • IMPORTANT TIP It is highly recommended that you know how to hack before you start fighting phases.
  • GET READY
  • A. Get ~150,000 ryo (10,000 to summon the phase and the rest to load up on Drain & Tonic juice)
  • B. Get enough core (generally 50,000-100,000 to pay 30k in bonuses (+5 drain, +10 edge) and hack costs)
  • Regular Phase
    • Core: 30,000 to 41,000
    • Stamina for hacks: 0 to 140
    • Hacks: 0-2 Hacks depending on your base Drain Str and the phases Drain Diff
    • Your drain str should be one less than the phase's drain diff
  • Drain Phase
    • Core: 80,000
    • Stamina for hacks: 350
    • Hacks: 5 in a fully upgraded village
    • Your drain str should be one less than the phase's drain diff
  • D. Fight/Drain the Phase
    • Always fight at Max Push unless you have 1 life left and a long way to go
    • Use Detective Duo for maximum damage
    • Always have at least 2 lives while attacking in order to avoid risking instant death (the 11 Tails takes two lives per hit, so keep 3 lives for that phase!)
    • Life sources are:
    • Legacy Bloodline (permanent +1 life)
    • Twilight Guardian (+1 life while it's active, make sure to resummon after dayroll!)
    • Storm Favor (+1 life, once per phase)
    • Zawa Zawa (+1 life, once per dayroll, may expire at end of day like Twilight Guardian)
    • Offline Romance (+1 life, team bonus)
  • Tips for Draining
  • Watch how much damage you do each swing, or better yet, sim it out using the Phase simulator
  • When the phase might go negative on your next hit, STOP!
  • Do one of the following:
    • Sim out what push % you should use to bring the phase between 1 and 99 HP
    • Guess what push % you should use and pray (not recommended)
    • Switch to the Noob Slayer team: you can now only do 50 HP per swing regardless of push. (Switch to minimum push at this point: no point having a higher death chance!)
