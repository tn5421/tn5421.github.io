This is the BVSwiki page for my 3 BVS Characters "KuriNoBito, UnmeiNoIto, & 4242564".
I'm only posting their stats, not their items, etc…

KuriNoBito - My Oldest Character
KuriNoBito is Japanese for "Angel of Darkness" (Kuri = Darkness, Dark) (No = of) (Bito = Angel)
I know I could of used (Tenshi = Angel), but Bito sounded better in my opinion.

UnmeiNoIto - My Second Oldest Character
UnmeiNoIto is Japanese for "Threads of Fate" (Unmei = Fate) (No = of) (Ito = Threads)
or it can be interpreted as (String of Fate). This was my Sister's Character, but she quit her character, so
i'm continuing it. So this is a Female Character, while the other 2 are Male Characters.

4242564 - My Newest Character
4242564 isn't just a bunch of random numbers, but it's from the anime "Soul Eater"
42-42-564 (shini-shini-goroshi) which translates to "Die-Die-Kill" or "Death-Death-Murder".
in the anime "Soul Eater" it's the Shinigami's Phone number, lol. It's an interesting number combo so I
made it intoa name, especially since BVS has the Bleach parody, with Shinigami (Soul Reapers), and
Death Note Parody, with Hyuk (Ryuk on Death Note), which is a Shinigami also, so this Shinigami would be
another for the list, lol. Thus, I have the Image of Shinigami for the avatar but with the "Dark Village"'s
head protector on, lol.
