Character Description

A small cat-like creature with white fur, pink eyes, fluffy tail, and a second set of long ears (which carry golden rings that apparently float around these) that come out of the first set of cat-like ears. A red oval ring is drawn on the fur on Kyuubee's back. It's face seems to be forever frozen in a smiling, blank stare.

Kyuubee can understand human languages perfectly, and communicates in the same language via telepathy.

History

First spotted in Hakurei Village, this creature was supposedly drawn in by the energy generated by the flow of village contracts being submitted beyond the Border, and started receiving ninja training. The reasons for it's fascination with these written parchments is not known at this point, as Kyuubee will dodge the subject everytime it's brought up. It's goals, likewise, are also unknown.

Kyuubee has made a brief passage through Pulsar Village in the past, being supposedly present at the time the village was destroyed. No clues towards it's involvement in this have been noted.
