Character Description

Tall, dark and handsome, of course… or so he tries to pull it off.
He could actually -be- tall, dark and handsome, after all there isn't much of his body to be seen.. he is most often seen wearing black clothing, tight enough to allow for swift moving but loose enough to hide a wide range of items, weapons and varied knick-knacks.
Occasionally, he has been known to pull livestock out of his sleeves too, but that's another story alltogeter.
The most notable speck of color is the long, dark purple muffler he wears loosely around his neck, almost always raised to cover his mouth and nose as well.
When asked why he wears such a weird piece of clothing, his only answer is an enigmatic smile (it's weird.. people always seem to -know- when he smiles… and it's not always a pleasant sensation) immediately followed by an:

It matches well with my eyes, don't you agree?

His eyes.. several shades lighter than many others from his own bloodline and, in fact, not red at all when watched from the right perspective: they are a very soft shade of violet, hinting perhaps at more than a trace of White Eye blood.
If he knows anything, he won't tell.

History

…I'm -so- not asking the creepy guy. You're on your own here, pal.

Well, now… aren't we getting curious, wanting to know my history, young ninja?
Ha, that's fun, actually.. there's much to tell, and very little willingness on my part to share my prized secrets… what would be a ninja without secrets, after all?
An unarmed fighter and a witless diplomat, an artisan without tools… something useless, broken, to be crushed and thrown away… but I guess I can share a bit about myself… just for you

You can feel an amused smile as his voice gets lower, almost a soft whisper, and he leans closer to you, words rolling in your ears like oil on silk.

I'm a ninja.. I steal and get back, kidnap and save, kill and avenge… I'm a giver of dreams and nightmares, depending on the side you ask.
Occasionally, I'm even more than that.

His hand runs absent-mindedly to the plain-looking katana resting at his hip, and his eyes get clouded with thought

…but you didn't ask for poetics. I come from a fairly big village, but I doubt you ever heard of it, it's hidden well, and in a far-away land.
Shortly after passing with honors my chunin exam, I got paired up with another promising ninja, Toby, and sent to scout and find out suitable places for an outpost in these lands.
As you can see, we found it, but by then we got at… odds… with our old leaders, and cut ourselves out from their influence.
Since then, with very short breaks inbetween, the mantle of leadership rests on my shoulders… and that's all you're getting out of me today, you curious kid.
Shoo, and be thankful
