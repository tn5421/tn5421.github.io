Starting Bloodlines

Season 1 players get to choose their starting bloodline when they reach level 9 by completing the Special Mission The Power of Blood. Season 2+ players choose their new bloodline when they loop.

RedEye

Definition: Learn enemy Jutsu

You poked yourself in the eye, and it bled all over.

The RedEye Bloodline grants the following abilities:

  • Lets you learn jutsu from missions through the use of Red Eye Jutsu for 25% of the original jutsu XP cost (Your RedEye whirls..).
  • You can learn RedEye-specific jutsu.
  • You get a AP/XP bonus while doing regular mission:
    • 7% as a Genin
    • 14% as a Chunin
    • 21% as a Special Jonin
    • 28% as a Jonin
    • 35% as a Sannin
  • You have a chance to find out the Type (Gen/Nin/Tai) and the Difficulty of the next round in Bingo Battles.
  • Shows location of one Random Symbol in Scratchies game.
  • You automatically use Infinite Awareness, which most villages own, to always level your allies.

This Bloodline is based on the Sharingan from Naruto.

WhiteEye

Definition: Find rare Ninja and Items more easily

You got something nasty in your eye, and it stayed that way.

The WhiteEye Bloodline grants the following abilities:

  • Allows you to see if a mission contains a hidden item, ninja, or event (The WhiteEye senses something..).
  • Doubles your chances for finding rare items, ninja, and events through the use of White Eye Jutsu.
  • You can learn WhiteEye-specific jutsu.
  • You get bonus levels during regular missions:
    • +1 Level as a Genin
    • +2 Levels as a Chunin
    • +3 Levels as a Special Jonin
    • +4 Levels as a Jonin
    • +5 Levels as a Sannin
  • You get one extra pull on Potion ingredients Hunts
    • Forest
    • BurgerNinja Dumpster
  • Shows location of x1 Bonuses in Scratchies game.

This Bloodline is based on the Byakugan from Naruto.

Legacy of the Hero

Definition: 50% more Chakra produced

You got a wicked eyepatch, just like the one in the legends!

The Legacy Bloodline grants the following abilities:

  • 50% more Chakra than other ninja.
  • You can learn Legacy-specific jutsu.
  • You can tame Super Frog.
  • You get +1 life when fighting phases.
  • You get a bonus Stamina Daily:
    • 15 as a Genin
    • 30 as a Chunin
    • 45 as a Special Jonin
    • 60 As a Jonin
    • 75 as a Sannin
  • You get 30 bonus Appetite daily.
  • Shows location of the Billy Bonus Spot in Scratchies game.

This Bloodline is based on the special abilities that Naruto has from the Naruto series.

Special Bloodlines

Blood of the Reaper / True Blood of the Reaper

Description: Willingly take penalties for added power

Change your stats through the Reaper Menu

This bloodline has 3 levels:

  1. Shikai (-2 Levels, +2 Strength) - acquired by completing the Quest Over the Edge
    • At this Level you get the jutsu Soul Reaper Style: < insert call >, < insert name > by completing the Quest Release the Power. This jutsu loops.
    • At this point the bloodline is called Blood of the Reaper.
  2. Bankai (+5 Levels, -1 Strength) - acquired by completing the Quest Adventures in the Other Side.
    • At this point the bloodline is called True Blood of the Reaper.
  3. Cobra Kai (-4 Levels, +4 Range) - acquired by completing the Quest The Pants or The Pantsless.

To acquire this bloodline you can use:

  • The Reaper Guide
  • The Monochrome Guide

The Trade

Description: Gain Insight into Bingo Attempts

This bloodline has 3 levels:

  1. Level 1 is acquired by completing the Quest The Trade. At this level it grants you:
    • You can give 2 Juicy Apples to see who is bingo'ed in a Village at that time.
    • If your village has the upgrade Tools of the Trade you can give 100 Ryo per level to become immune to Note Pages for 24 hours.
    • You can see the attribute and difficulty during a Bingo Battle.
    • You get 10 Stamina after a successful bingo.
    • -10 daily Stamina.
  2. Level 2 is acquired by completing the Quest Outwitting the Best. At this level it grants you:
    • The Note - Gives the Note Rush (+20% AP/XP/Ranking/Ryo) on a successful Bingo.
    • +20 Stamina for each successful bingo
    • -20 daily Stamina (this includes the Level 1 penalty)
  3. Level 3 is acquired by completing the Quest Sick of Him!. At this level it grants you:
    • The Tote - If you bingo a Leader with a Note Page, you have +10 Range to Attack that Village
    • +30 Stamina for each successful bingo
    • -30 daily Stamina (this includes the Level 1 and Level 2 penalty)

To acquire this bloodline you can use The Trade Guide.

Special Note: This bloodline does not give you stamina back if you decide to do a Hard Bingo.

Wasteland Disease / Not Wasteland Disease

Description: No Strength Bonuses in Wasteland Missions1 / Only +1 Strength in Wasteland Missions, enter the Inner Wastes2

This bloodline has 2 levels:

  1. Wasteland Disease is acquired by completing the Quest Revenge.
    • It unlocks Outskirts missions.
  2. Not Wasteland Disease is acquired by completing the Quest Second Opinion.
    • Unlocks high-level Wasteland missions.
    • +1 Strength in the Wasteland.

To acquire this bloodline you can use The Wasteland Guide.

Eye of the Storm

Description: Call upon the power of The Storm

This bloodline is acquired by completing the Quest Eye of the Storm.

  • You can activate this bloodline by going to the Storm Menu in which you will have 6 favors to choose from. To activate it you need to have favors. These are acquired by getting 7500+ storm points.
  • This bloodline is not lost when looping.
  • You may get 2 storms when your storm speed erupts by having Limitless Focus Level 3

To acquire this bloodline you can use this guide The Truth / Eye of the Storm.

The Power of Greass

Description: Trade your Stamina for automatic successes

Behold the Power of Greass!

This bloodline is acquired by completing the Quest Shift Manager of Doom.
It gives you a range and level bonus equal to your crank and automatically gives any available Items / Allies / Events possible if you win the Mission. Having the bloodline prevents you from losing the extra 5 Stamina on BurgerNinja missions.

Works once a day, only once per Mission per Season, uses all Stamina. The checkbox for this is near the bottom of the screen where you are choosing what jutsu to use.
However:

  • The get the item part now works on all events, items and allies. Field keys are items.
  • Doing this sets your stamina to zero, no matter what it was before you clicked this box. You may use it only one time per day even if you replenish your stamina/get a mission after you have used it.
  • You may only do this in a given season a number of times equal to four + number of seasons you have completed (i.e. S1 can use it 5 times, S2 can use it 6, etc.) The checkbox will tell you how many more times you can use it.
  • DO NOT USE WITH ESCAPE JUTSU. YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED.
  • You may only use this on a given mission once per season (i.e. you can use it in say Rock Out On Stage to get Emo Rock CDs, but then if you get Rock Out On Stage again, you will not be able to Greass it.)
  • This bloodline is not lost when looping.

To acquire this bloodline you can use The BurgerNinja Guide.

Mister Roboto Bloodline

Description: Bonuses in the Robo Fighto

You have won [a Robo Fighto Tournament]! For coming in first, you receive the Mister Roboto Bloodline! Five percent off RF costs, and one free use of Beyond My Control per day.

This bloodline is acquired by coming in 1st Place in any Robo Fighto Tournament.

  • Robo Fighto Tournaments cost 5% less Ryo per entry
  • Can use Beyond My Control once per day to perform a Push with a 100% success rate
  • This bloodline does not loop

When you use the BMC Push in a Robo Tournament you get this message:

Beyond My Control! 100% Push Chance!
Himitsu wo shiri tai.

You may only use BMC once per attempt. If you try to use it a second time on a tourney that lasts through dayroll, you have the normal success chance for that push, burn your BMC use for the day, and get this message:

Beyond My Control FAILED! You've already BMC'ed this attempt!

Mysterious Power Bloodline

Chunin-Level Refer Bonus! You've referred 50 people who have made Chunin - you've won the Mysterious Power Bloodline!

This bloodline can be acquired by earning 50 referral points, looping to season 30, or buying the referral booster for 300 Karma(270 If BC Member).

  • If you aren't Hanging Out with anyone, you can Hang Out with 1 Ally of your choice!
  • This bloodline is not lost when looping.

Silver-Eye

Description: Claymore has double chance of activating.

Your Claymore begins calling to you in your mind, and a man walks up to you!
Check your Quests!

You are contacted by the Organization, for your work done here!
You get X Ryo!

This bloodline is acquired by completing the Quest Silver-Eyed Witch.

  • Doubles chance of Claymore activating.
  • Gives you Ryo upon a successful invasion (500 * Season of Kage up to 5000 Ryo).
  • This bloodline is not lost when looping.

Drift Style

Description: Can spend 300 Stamina to change Drift Style from the Main Menu

  • Racer's Edge
  • Shift to Drift

Description +10 Edge on normal missions

This Bloodline is acquired in the Quest The Witching Hour, after completing Easy mode in Pizzawitch (AKA Minimum Safe Distance)

Description: Take Range penalties in order to use them later

This Bloodline is acquired in the Quest The Witching Hour, after completing Crazy Hard mode in Pizzawitch (AKA Infiltration)

  • Allows you to take a -2 Range penalty in order to bank it for later
  • Up to +88 Range can be banked this way
  • MegaMissions bank 20 Range each, while still only costing -2 Range for the mission

Fate

This bloodline is acquired by completing the Quest Order.

  • Gives you 10 Pony Parade at the RNG Shack on the day of the Festival.
  • The item Pony Parade give +11% damage against Personal Army Worldkais.
  • You receive double bonuses on the quest, Watchin Your Shows.
  • Unlocks the Avatar Abilities menu on the main page
  • This bloodline loops
  • NOTE: You cannot have this Bloodline and the Destiny Bloodline at the same time

Destiny

This bloodline is acquired by completing the Quest Chaos.

  • Gives you 10 Pony Parade at the RNG Shack on the day of the Festival.
  • The item Pony Parade give +11% damage against Free For All Worldkais.
  • You receive double bonuses on the quest, Watchin Your Shows.
  • Unlocks the Avatar Abilities menu on the main page
  • This bloodline loops
  • NOTE: You cannot have this Bloodline and the Fate Bloodline at the same time

Heart of the Cards

This bloodline is acquired by completing the quest chain, starting with: It doesn't matter if you're Black or White 1, and ending with It doesn't matter if you're Black or White 4. You acquire this bloodline in It doesn't matter if you're Black or White 3

It is a four part quest, requiring the 4 Heart of the Cards Custom Kaiju Drops (from Zenovia, Jasticus, Jadian, & Fayt), The Glow, the Steel Mahjong Set, the Lycoral Hanafuda Deck, and the Special Attack Uniform.

  • This bloodline loops.
  • NOTE: This Bloodline was originally obtained with a specialty collection pack to help bring the card game, WeiÎ² Schwarz & Cardfight!! Vanguard into the United States and the Western Hemisphere.

Description: 11% chance per day to get a free play in all card-related areas.

  • Flower Wars
  • Mahjong
  • Scratchy Tickets
  • PizzaWitch Rides
  • Pigeons

This is not 11% chance per area, this is an all or nothing chance. Either you will get a free play in all of the above areas, or none.

Spiral Frequency

  • Lets you Tune your chakra to make certain specific Cave missions easier.
  • This bloodline loops.

Thousand-Yard Stare

  • 5% chance per Retail level to double-attack Kaiju/Worldkai
  • This bloodline loops.

The Pirate King

To acquire, you need to win a Tier 11 match in Number One, then go to the juice bar and drink rum.

  • 11% Megamission bonus and:
  • +1 Str in missions for WhiteEye
  • half Chakra costs on Missions/Bingos for RedEye
  • +50 Stamina per rank for Legacy
