Shikai
release command: Suraisu (slice)

Bankai
bankai name: Hoeru kazeryu (lit howl wind dragon)

abilities:
suraisu kaze: shoots a blast of reiatsu that can cut anything and uses little reiatsu when it has a focus weapon such as my blade. When i do not have blade it is weaker and requires more reiatsu. This ability is used automatically whenever i swing my blade.

inazuma:by using the air control i create friction making a static charge allowing me to send lightning at my enemies.

winston (guest) 16 Feb 2009 08:47

fang of the dragon.

unreleased is a regular katana, nothing special.

shikai- a kusari-gama, released with the call to "sharpen". the chain can be controlled by my reiatsu and extends quite a bit( not sure how far, never needed more than a few yards). when holding it by the handle i can compress my reiatsu through it to the blade at very high pressure, allowing the blade to cut nearly anything. this attack is called "bite".

bankai- released with the call "devour" the kusari-gama is largely unchanged except for gaining red and gold color to it, the major chainge is in the fact that the reiatsu emanating from my body is compressed into a suit of winged, super dense, red and gold armor. the armor isn't impenetrable, and can only hold up as long as my reiatsu holds out, but it's an incredible defense. i can use burst of compressed reiatsu to speed my movements, but that mostly only negates the drag that the armor causes, and in a pinch, i can use compressed bursts through my finger tips much like the "bite" attack. Upon the helm of the suit, there is a set of horns which increase my sensitivity to reiatsu, allowing me to somewhat anticipate the moves of my opponents( this skill is indispensable since most other bankai level fighters are quite a bit faster than me), and allows me to limit the flow of reiatsu in parts of the armor where it isn't needed.
