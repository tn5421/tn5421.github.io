Kaiju Point Standings
BvS-Uzuki 31 Aug 2008 15:03

These points are used for purchasing kaiju drops, when they become available. You can obtain points in the following ways:

Attacking a kaiju: +1 pt. per attack (top 10% of attackers on a kaiju get double points)
Spying on another village: +3 pt. per spy placed

For duplicate kaiju drops (all but 5 of them), the system will work like this. Base cost will be kaiju's health divided by 25. In the event that more than one person has enough points to buy the item, people should send me 1 message saying how much they're willing to spend on it. The item will go to the person who bid the most, but they will only have to pay the amount the second place bidder bid + 1 point (minimum of the item's cost). If that's a tie, it goes to whoever has more points then. If no one wants to buy an item with points, it goes in the claw machine.

You can still bid on items that are in the claw machine, assuming no one has won them yet. If someone bids the minimum bid for the item when it's in the claw machine, I take it out of the claw machine and send it to them. Otherwise, you're welcome to try and spend ryo to win it.
