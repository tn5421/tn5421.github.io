Anonymous (128.231.88.x) 02 Sep 2008 19:52

Honestly, there is no single "best team" for the biggest Wasteland Missions: you're going to have to tailor your team to beat them.

What CAN be useful, though, is having LisaLisa, Hyuk, and Right. You can't use them directly in the Wasteland, but you can use them to farm Note Pages. These are needed for LisaLisa's jutsu 'Turn the Page', which will let you switch teams without using Stamina. This makes switching teams in and out much easier.

Re: Best Team for Doing Wasteland Missions?
Buretsu 03 Sep 2008 03:42

Early Outskirts:
Flutie2, Sticky2, Haus. In Season 1, without access to the Stand By Theme, improving drop rates is a must. 4x Rate from Ichi Ramen + Haus is almost an auto-drop on most missions, and Tai missions are relatively rare, so Tubby isn't a must. Once you do get Stand By going, you can swap Tubby2 back in.

Advanced Outskirts, Early Wastelands:
Bones2, Haus, Good Boy. Good all around Range and free Successes, with increased Drop Rate. This will serve you well until you can unlock Advanced Wastelands. Plus, on Crank 0, with this setup and with Letter of Marque, Sword Call Jutsu is an auto-pass on the missions get the initial 3 Wastelands allies.

Advanced Wastelands:
If you're in need of range, go with The Paper, Venus, Jaws. A solid 5 Range and 1 added Strength. It's at this point you'll really want to consider Diagnosis to help with getting drops. Otherwise, you can continue on with the Bones2, Haus, Good Boy setup.
