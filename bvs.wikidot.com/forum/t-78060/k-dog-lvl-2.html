RikaCovena 08 Jan 2011 14:02

If it's anything B-rank or lower, I recommend betting about 8 emblems at once. Then you have a 72.7% chance of actually winning, but you are also using relatively fewer emblems, and thus can use a larger number of attempts. With S-ranks, storing up until you have a total of at least 10 for a 90.9% chance of success is recommended. Best would be 11, just so you don't risk failing on a S-Col. A-ranks i'd recommend at least 9, if not 10, just because you want those Tiny Bees.
