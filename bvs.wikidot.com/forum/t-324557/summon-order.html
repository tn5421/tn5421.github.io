Jasticus 14 May 2011 22:14

Clarification of summon order, as according to Fax:

Basically, like this:
1. Check to see if village has Kaiju Corpse. If no, summon first custom on list that village is missing an upgrade for. If none, summon Playerkai. Else, go to next.
2. Check to see if MONSTER was used and village has Elemental Beacon. If so, add majorkai to the pool. Else, go to next.
3. Check to see if village has all kaiju upgrades. If no, summon randomly from pool of missing upgrades. (Only applies if village has never had upgrade before.) Else, go to next.
4. Failing that, summon a random kaiju from the pool.

Prem-aka-Prince 25 Jun 2011 14:15

Not exactly. PlayerKai is just short for Player Kaiju, which is any player that got a kaiju, typically speaking that from the 'impossible mission'. Custom Kaiju are the list of players that got special kaiju and that's what makes the summon order. Whether or not it's smart to call customkai a subset of playerkai is not important… just gotta know the difference. Sometimes people on the wiki will have made mistakes. Some of these pages are very old so they may have been fine at the time but are unclear now. If you see something that needs updating, go ahead…
Vampires, etc, are just non-player Minor Kaiju. All player kaiju and custom kaiju so far are also minor kaiju, but they're not simply the same thing.
