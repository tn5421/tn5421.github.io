Buretsu 24 Jul 2008 19:01

At Season 1, your Max Crank is 0. Each additional Season adds 2 to the Maximum Crank. So, in Season 2, you can have Crank 2. Season 3, you can get Crank 4. And so on.

Each Crank Level adds +1d/+1s to non-Special missions. Crank 1 is +1/+1. Crank 4 would be +4/+4. Et cetera. Also, there is a bonus of +5% Ryo per Crank Level. And, since difficulty of a mission determines the AP/XP Gains of a mission, those increase with every crank.

Crank Levels also affect Item Drops and Ally Levelups. Anything that previously only required S2+ (Like several of the Level 2 allies, like Spot2 and Terri3, or items like the Goo Stabilization Recipe) now also requires you to be at least Crank Level 1. Anything that previously only required S3+ (Like Terri3 or Sandman2) now require you to be at least Crank Level 3.
