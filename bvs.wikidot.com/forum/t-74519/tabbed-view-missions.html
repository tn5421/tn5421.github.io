I was just thinking that a tabbed view for missions might be niceâ€¦though I had a bit of a different idea in mind for the organization.

Convenience is second to functionality; if a good chunk of users won't be able to use the wiki because the information is put in tabs, then they won't be implemented, simple as that. If we find that the tabbed view is sufficiently compatible, though, I don't see why we shouldn't do this for the missions page and possibly other pages as well.
