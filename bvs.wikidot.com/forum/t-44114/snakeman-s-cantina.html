SNAKEMAN's Cantina
BvS-Uzuki 29 Feb 2008 19:12

A fragment of a newspaper tumbles across the path. It bears a headline, "Mysterious Tavern Discovered!" Looking closer, the following story can be discovered…

Deep within the forest of the Country of Sandwiches, an old run down bar has been found. It appears that a large number of shinobi participating in the Ninja World War have been congregating here for quite some time. The sign outside the door reads "SNAKEMAN's Cantina," suggesting that this may be the establishment of the notorious Sannin level criminal. Shinobi are advised to take extreme caution before approaching this establishment.

(Note: Feel free to use this thread to roleplay amongst yourselves. It's a standard "bar" style environment, so knock yourselves out!)
