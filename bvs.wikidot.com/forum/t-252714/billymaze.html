Buretsu 12 Jul 2010 05:44

There are 2 keys to successfully completing the maze:
1) Write down each correct direction. The right way never changes except from month to month.
2) Write down which direction you entered the room from, and which of the 5 options that direction is.

For example:
you enter Area 1 from the South..
East West South Ninja North

Remember South, and that the 3rd option was South. This is EXTREMELY important for getting out of the maze.

There are 11 Areas. Once you reach Area 11, you snatch the prize and then have to escape from the maze. You're automatically sent back to Area 10. Once you get there, though… ALL text will have changed to a string of AAAAAA.

The way to get out is to follow the directions you entered the area from.

If you entered Area 10 from the East, you need to go East, which means you also need to know which of the 5 AAAA directions actually IS East. Repeat this 10 times, and you've escaped the maze.

Which means the REAL hard part is figuring out the first 10 Directions through trial and error.
