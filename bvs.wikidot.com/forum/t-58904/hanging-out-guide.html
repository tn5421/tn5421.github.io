BvS-Uzuki 11 May 2008 15:28

I'm not sure hanging out really needs a guide, there's not a whole lot to it. Hanging out allows you to learn a person's jutsu for half the JXP cost and use it that day for zero chakra cost. It also lets you pass certain missions that require you be hanging out with an ally. There's four ways to hang out with an ally:

First, you can have high friend points with that ally. (If the highest friend point holder has < 5000, 1 person gets to hang. 5,000-24,999 two people, 25,000-49,999 three people, and +1 for every 25k after that). You get friend points every time you run a mission. To maximize friend points, you simple stack friend point bonuses (J-Diddy is 4x, TicTac is 2x, Battle of Champions theme is 2x, Ninja-Mas Spirit jutsu is 2x, and the item LoveLove Paradise is 2x), and then run missions until you're near the top.

Second way to do it is with a Make-out Mood Enhancer. You set it up so there's only one person in your party, then drink the potion, and you're hanging out. You can get up to 2 this way.

Third is with Floating on Air. Set your opening theme to Floating, then you'll be hanging out with all three allies in your party from overnight. You can get up to 3 this way.

Fourth is the Mysterious Power Bloodline. If you refer 50 people, you can hang out with 1 ally per day for free if you're not currently hanging out with anyone. Max you can hang out with this way is 1.

That's it, that's all the guide would have.

Jadian 14 Jun 2008 10:51

I'm fairly certain the base amount of friend points per mission is 25 with a 3-man team and 40 with a 2-man team…I haven't used only 1 ally in a month or two. Type of mission and mission difficulty have no bearing on the friend point gain, except for the missions that have +xxx friend points hidden.

The max that can be earned from any mission using a 3-man team is 1600 friend points with the set-up Uzuki described. A-rank and AA-rank missions have a mission with 4000 friends hidden, as well.

Also, since hanging out was recently changed to minimum of 3000 friend points, I'll swing by to update that on the Allies page…
