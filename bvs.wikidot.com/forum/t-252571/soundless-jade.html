Ren Po Ken 10 Jul 2010 20:53

Pizza Witch is literally unbeatable without doing r00t first. You can beat it the easy way (I did), but the hard way requires you to have The Eleven Tails.

There are exactly three allies in the entire game who do anything in fields. Stalker 3 (+1 Stamina in fields), K.Y. 2 (+25% Core), and Doughmon 2/3 (Access to Bitshop 2). None of them will help you beat up discords, but you should use this team for it's other benefits.

Do not count on the Twilight Guardian. I was never able to get a discord to show up before it evaporated.

Now for some good news. There is a r00t recompiling calculator for windows developed by blueaddict. There is also a spreadsheet that accomplishes the same thing by catprog.

Don't bother recompiling much past a 20% or so chance of success. The discords show up often enough that you can afford a 1 in 5 win rate.
