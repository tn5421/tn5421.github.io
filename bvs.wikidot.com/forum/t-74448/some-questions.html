MRUhlir 15 Jul 2008 03:46

One reason why you're failing missions is you're range. Many B-rank missions have a difficultly of greater then 10, which is as high as you can go in Gen and Nin. Buy Chakra Armor and try to win a K-Dog Headband to increase your range. To increase the chances of passing right away use jutsu that increase range, such as Kido's taught by Shorty(which also give you successes), or Lil' Bo's Welcome to Good Burger. You should also try to get as much strength as possible. And as Uzuki said, Ro-Sham-Bo Redux is probably the best team you can use that doesn't require already being able to beat B-ranks(such as Crazy Love Triangle).

Lear 15 Jul 2008 04:53

The Ro-Sham-Bo Redux is probably the ideal team for a Season 1 Sp. Jonin, at least until you have Crazy Love Triangle (Billy Lvl.2, Pinky Lvl.2, Stalkergirl Lvl.2).

I should also point out that Sp. Jonin is probably the ideal time to start the Reaper Missions: http://bvs.wikidot.com/guide:reaper

You ought to be strong enough that you can use Epic Dog Urination Technique (which you WILL need) on the missions to get the Heck Butterfly, Swallowtail Butterfly, and Soul Glove and you should be strong enough for most (not all, but most) missions you'll encounter as a Sp. Jonin. You'll need to be Jonin to finish the Reaper missions, but Sp. Jonin's not a bad place to start.

Also, just because B ranks are available to you now doesn't mean you have to take them. You can still run C ranks while farming for ryo to buy items or farming for item drops. There's nothing wrong with that either.

Jadian 15 Jul 2008 10:31

Unless you have something giving you range to pass B-ranks, you probably won't be able to get Billy, Pinky, and Stalkergirl to lvl 2 very easily. All three of them require that you take them along on their B-rank level up mission several times before they'll level. The only one you can probably level up right now is Billy, as he levels on a Genjutsu mission, while Pinky and Stalkergirl level up on Tai and Nin missions respectively. Ro-Sham-Bo Redux is the way to go until you have the range to be able to get Crazy Love Triangle.

Also, all of the items that you can purchase with NWW points will be useful to you, though right now I would probably suggest getting the Medals ( +1 Strength in that stat ) first.
