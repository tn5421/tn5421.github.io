On Monsters
BvS-Uzuki 16 Jun 2008 22:03

Does anyone object to a rule that everyone in the village has to attack at least once a day if there's currently a kaiju alive? Failure to do so could result in the loss of access to upgrades / storehouse.

Kaiju are seriously nasty now. The Fox Demon is reported to have 30,000 health (in addition to it's regenerating and increasing in strength). I imagine the Sand Demon is the same. Expect the Storm Riders and other 20k monsters to have 30,000 at this point. I don't think we can toy around with them like we used to.

However, I still think we need to be killing monsters regularly (including enticement monsters). We still don't have Super Robots, which are Enticement only, and their upgrade gives 20% off arena reputation items, which is huge. We really need to keep enticing until we get that upgrade, at the very least.

However, I'm not willing to summon them if they're going to start being a real threat to our upgrades. So, the alternative to having a rule that says "You must attack once a day." is to just stop summoning monsters. I'd rather not do this, but if you guys are really uncomfortable with the "You have to attack once a day" rule, then going without summons might be the best way to go.
