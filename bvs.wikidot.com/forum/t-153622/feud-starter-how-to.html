im trying to get the feud starter, and aim for the top throphys, but i suck at bingoing… any suggestions on how to go about doing that???… like how much stamina is needed, best team to use, and items, or any other tip would be awesome,
Thanx a lot

Re: feud-starter (how to)
1337 (guest) 05 May 2009 18:25

1. Get the Tote, or at very least the Note. This will save you a lot of stamina. (it'll cost about 600 stamina with the Tote, about 900 with the Note.)
2. If you have the Seasons Don't Fear the Reaper theme, put it on.
3. Always try to aim at people below your level.
4. Don't try to bingo kages, since they tend to have more bingo defenses up than an regular player.
5. If you're not in a rush, stock up on note pages, and cure right wounds (or get a full moon if you have loyal sacrifice)
