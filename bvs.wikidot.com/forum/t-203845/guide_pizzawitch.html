jediwaldo 14 Dec 2009 16:37

I didnt want to steal someone's thunder with this page. But so far i have written a guide to PW that is a little more indepth and less redundant.
Guide to PizzaWitch
Requirements:
Gen/Nin/Tai
Levels: 35+20
Strength: 10+
Range: 36+

Doujutsu
Levels: 25+
Strength: 0
Range: 18 (prefer 20+)

Steps to Pass Pizzawitch the Unlock Quest:
Quest Checks:
Doujutsu 20 difficulty, 10 successes
Doujutsu 10 difficulty, 500 rolling successes
Need to get to 20 range first.
Use Team of Lulu + Su-chan for a total of:
* +2 Doujutsu Levels
* +4 Doujutsu Range
* +1 Doujutsu Strength
Eat 2-4 Greassy Fries for 2-4 more range
Eat 1-2 Greassy Nuggets for 1-2 more strength
Use rest of appetite to eat Tasty Pizzas
All the above can be skipped and replaced with a Greassy Royales.
Once you are into PW missions and have compelted a few, Using all Yummy Pizzas is recommended and a team of Lulu lvl.2 and Su-chan lvl.2
Lulu Mission: Be in an Advertisement
Dou 15 diff, 25 Suc
Nin 30diff, 27 suc
Tai 34 diff, 25 suc
Su Chan Mission: Tell Everyone about PizzaWitch
Dou 20diff, 15 suc
Nin 25diff, 30 suc
Tai 35diff, 20 suc

Unlocking Rides

To unlock rides, you must complete the quest Behind the Wheel.
This quest becomes available once youâ€™ve obtained the Pizzawitch Uniform and completed 50 PizzaWitch missions.
To complete the quest, you must have obtained:
Lulu 2
Su-chan 2
Polyester Delivery Jacket
Scuffed Delivery Hat
Tattered Driving Gloves
Tacky Keychain from PizzaWitch missions.
R00t Password

Once you complete the quest, you will receive the item License to Speed and unlock PizzaWitch deliveries in the Village menu.

Deliveries:
You are limited to 4 deliveries a day with no booster or awesome in rides.
Go ahead and do the introduction to deliveries quest until you pass it.
Once you do that.
Do Easy Deliveries until you have upgraded your stats to your rides max (drive 3/drift 3/ destruction 3) and you start out at 2/2/2.
Once you have upgraded your stats, go ahead and complete dogfight delivery to unlock normal deliveries.
Once you have beat Dogfight, go ahead and farm up 30,000 tips to get the Ninja Wagon ride. (The Magic bus will become useful after you have more than 2 passengers.)

Quest: SuperSpy (Karen get)
Requirements: License to Speed, Access to Easy Rides
You will need to be hanging out with Lulu and have over 100 awesome.
It has a check of:
dou 10diff, 10succ
nin 10diff, 10succ

Quest: Shower Scene (Karen lvl. 2 until dayroll)
Requirements: Lulu in team or hanging out with Karen
Simple quest no further explanation

Quest: Grind
Requirements: Karen, Cici, Access to Normal Rides
You need also:
Over 200 awesome
I have 20 Sharpening Stones and 40 Metal Scraps
Also the quest has a dou 10d10s + tai 15d15s check
Rewards the Item: Grind Core which gives -1 successes.
This item quest/item unlocks high level PW missions which is then used an ingredient for a level 5 item upgrade. Also, gives access to most of the rest of the PW allies.

Buretsu 15 Mar 2010 19:03

Once you have access to the PizzaWitch menu, one of the options is Make Pizzas.

In order to make them, you need Ingredients, which you get by appraising the PW Ally Drops (Frost-Covered Packages, etc). Pizzas which you have the ingredients to make will show up in a list, along with how many of each Ingredient they require.
