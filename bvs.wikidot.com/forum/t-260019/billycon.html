Buretsu 22 Aug 2010 08:45

Stuffs are permanent and don't go away, however they have to be Packed, and space is limited. (Though there is at least 1 Stuffs that increases how much you can pack)

Most give permanent effects, but some give single-use effects like Energy Drinks (which works like the Extra Noms/Clothes) or the Credit Card (which can be used to bank Monies for a later con, or retrieve said Monies).
