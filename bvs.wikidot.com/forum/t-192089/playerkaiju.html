Jan 31st 2011:
I found that a lot of the data is available here: http://bvs.ecansol.com/kaiju/
They are missing a few and #169 on it is not an actual immortal kaiju but a regular player who hasn't completed the impossible mission, but most of the info is correct and all of it is verifiable if you look up the names they give.

I noticed that someone has added 175 without putting placeholders for 150 through 174 so they have been skipped thus far. I added them and went through the entire list to make sure there aren't any other missing. I was doing it rather quickly but I believe no other numbers have been skipped. Please put placeholders instead of skipping numbers.

Clarified "Immortal Realm" to "Kaiju of Immortal Realm". (at least) one person put down his current village which he did not found. Immortal Realm is special because once founded the Kage can never leave it for another village, and can never relinquish Kageship to another, it is permanent. Examining the data it seems that almost all were being put down as None if they were not the Kage of the village they were in. The combination of prior evidence and this logic lead me to deduce that it is intended to list founded IM not whatever village you are currently in, and to rename it appropriately.

I have finished going over any Kaiju who does not have an immortal realm listed, if they were in a village I looked it up to see if it was an immortal realm with them as a the kage. I updated the info on the correct ones.
I also added all known Kaiju up to 298 (the latest as of today). And I entered all the info posted in the comments made before this one.
