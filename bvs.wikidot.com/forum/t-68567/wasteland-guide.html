Getting Bones

Once you have any of the following: Flutie, Tubby, Sticky, The Twins, a special mission will appear, called "Rock Out".

Doing this special mission with any of the four in your team will level them up to level 2, but they will delevel at dayroll.

Leveling all four on the same day will unlock the special mission: Battle of the Bands (http://bvs.wikidot.com/mission:battle-of-the-bands)

The mission requires one roll of tickets, Emosuke level 2/3, and has a d16/s16 part, so it is recommended to bring tubby along as well as learn his justu.
