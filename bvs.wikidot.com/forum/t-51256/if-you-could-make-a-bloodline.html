Ren Po Ken 04 Apr 2008 15:29

RingEye
This is not a selectable bloodline, you would have to earn it like the Reaper Bloodline.

After going Ronin for 30 days as a Jonin and having completed 30 S-Rank missions, you are allowed to join the Akasuki in a special mission. This will be a big multipart mission with many sub-missions, one of which will be capture (bingo) someone who has either one of the Kaiju themes or an item that gives you a theme (may change this to only someone with one or both themes. Garra was a season 2 character when he was captured after all.). The ability to bingo would be granted from the Special Mission theme, and only those with the theme/spirits would be available.

The bingo is not a normal bingo either. While the victim would not loose their theme or item, Red Water would not reduce bingo time, and they couldn't use Cure Right Wounds. When you complete this mission, you would get Extracted Spirit (of whichever one you get). Another factor, for seventy two hours, the victim's village will have the ability to bingo you despite the fact that you're not in a village (A special Ronin 'village' would appear in their bingo screen) Also the use of note pages in both the first bingo and retaliatory bingos would be forbidden.

This Extracted Spirit is given to the Akasuki leader, Hurt, in the next and penultimate special mission of this section. You would then have to beat up Hurt in the final special mission and pluck his eye out and put in your socket.

What the RingEyeactually does:

  • +3.5% AP/XP per Rank (cumulative with Redeye). Does not round.
  • +.5 Levels per rank on regular missions (round up, cumulative with WhiteEye)
  • Ringeye: Innate Jutsu, 100 Chakra, Effects of Lvl 1 WhiteEye or RedEye as appropriate.
  • RingEye does not level (so you will not get +1 Str and/or +2 Lvl from using
  • You would receive the occasional insight when bingoing like a Redeye does (but it's only half as likely to occur).
  • RingEye does not give you access to WhiteEye or Redeye only jutsu (but if you all ready were a White/Red Eye, you don't loose them).
  • Jutsu with an Elemental effect (Fire Style: Whatever, Blizzard Jutsu, The Shocker, probably others) will cost half chakra. Reaper jutsu would not be affected even if the name or description implied an element (like Archer Style: Fire and Flame jutsu).

This is a reference to Pain or Tobi (I forget which) from Naruto.

Another Bloodline I would make
Hands of God

  • In exchange for 100 Stamina, you would unbingo someone.
  • There would be a board advertising your services, with a list of fees (most likely two fees, as you would probably want to give people in your village a discount) in Ryo. You set your own fee, with a minimum of 10,000 Ryo.
  • Once a patient clicks on your name in the board, the fee would be deducted, you would loose 100 Stamina, and the patient would be unbingoed.
  • Once your stamina dips below 100, your name is removed from the board until next dayroll. Your name will not be returned earlier, even if you raise your stamina level above 100.
  • A Kage can allow someone to draw on Village Ryo rather than their own. This would be done via a permission.

You would also get an ally with this bloodline, Tumor (may form a team with Sicko) who would reduce the Stamina loss for unbingoing a patient to 75.

This bloodline is a Blackjack reference, rather than a Trauma Center reference.

Altered Nova 04 Apr 2008 21:22

That RingEye bloodline sounds WAY overpowered… seriously, it practically obsoletes both WhitEye and RedEye. I realize you're trying to be accurate to the source material (in Naruto, the Rin'negan actually does obsolete both of those bloodlines), but that particular absurdly powerful bloodline is one that probably shouldn't ever make it into the game.

I'd either weaken the bloodline by limiting it to it's other effect - mastery of all six elements (giving a large chakra discount to all elemental jutsu known) and giving it some powerful unique bloodline jutsu. Either that, or make this bloodline limited to season 3+.

Some possible unique jutsu (assuming you capture Hurt to use as an ally after gaining the bloodline):

  • The Six Paths of Hurt - +6 levels, +6 strength, +6 range, +6 successes : 600 C. This is Based on Pein's "The Six Path's of Pain" technique, in which he can summon up to 5 clones to battle by his side. These clones are unique in that they aren't formed from chakra, they are literal genetic clones of Pain's former allies, grown in a lab, and they all share one mind with him. He can control them flawlessly, they all share eyesight, and they are apparently immortal, as the only way to defeat Pain is presumably to kill him and every clone simultaneously.
  • Illusionary Dragon Nine Consuming Seals - +10 range vs. Monsters, 400 C. If used against the Demon of the Sand or the Nine-Tailed Fox, there is a small chance (increasing with the number of times used) that you will obtain the Spirit of the Demon of the Sand or the Spirit of the Nine-Tailed Fox upon the monster's defeat, even if you're not the Kage. This is obviously based on the jutsu Pein used to extract the bijuu from their hosts and seal them into that large statue.
  • Summons - Pein has used at least six different summons in the manga, so you could allow the player to learn several of them from him. I'd probably use that Giant Chameleon one, it was pretty cool cause it could turn invisible. Not sure how that would work mechanically though.

The Hand of God Bloodline is pretty sweet too, but I'd give the players the ability to remove themselves from the advertising board if they don't want to sell their services for a while, and blacklist certain villages or players that they don't want to help. Also, they could set a maximum daily limit to the number of times other people could buy their services, if they don't want to find themselves at less than 100 stamina every time they log in. Also can we really call it a bloodline? Technically, it's a profession, not some genetic ability. But then again, I don't think The Trade quites makes sense that way either.

You'd have to set up a hell of a special mission line for this Bloodline though. It's not exactly easy to become a world-famous mercenary doctor!

Is Tumor supposed to be Blackjack? I would figure you'd give a card related name, like Poker or Pontoon. I'd make his bonus be if you are bingo'd while he's in your party, recovery time is halved. What kind of jutsu (if any) would he teach you though?

Also, what kind of bonus do you think Hurt would give you, and would he team with anyone?
