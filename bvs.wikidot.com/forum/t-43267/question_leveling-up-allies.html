It's giving me a message that it looks like he got stronger, but that he hasn't leveled up yet. Does this mean I failed the level up roll? Or is Timmy gaining experience?

Re: Question: Leveling up Allies
BvS-Uzuki 28 Feb 2008 04:54

Nah, you shouldn't even need the powerful potion once you get Tats + Shikai. Before today, I was using powerful potions to try the mission, and they worked, but you were limited to one per day and since it's a roll, just doing it once isn't going to cut it.

I'm having no problem completing the mission now with Tats+Shorty+Timmy while using Kido:Iron Maiden. I'm only a special jonin (nin), and I've got the following items which help: Badge of Wisdom (+1 Gen level), Substitute Soul Reaper Badge (+1 Gen Strength), Ring of Power (+1 Tai Success), Sword of the Reaper (+2 levels), Soul Glove (+1 Level), Helper Pin (+1 Level), Coolness Badge (+2 Levels), Reaper Blood: Shikai (-4 levels, +2 strength), and Medal of Power (+1 Strength). I think I've got like an 80-90% chance of passing the mission with this setup.

You don't need this setup…there's other things you can do. Get a Trench Knife, Desert Robes, Chakra Armor, K-Dog's Headband, Necklace of the HoCage, Badge of Power, Medal of Wisdom, etc would all help, and I don't have any of that stuff.

Anyway, it's not as hard as say, Billy Lvl. 3 level up.

If you want to see your chances, use this calculator to determine the probability of passing the mission. It's Tai10d7/Gen8d8 for the mission. Plug in stats and allies and see what'll maximize your chances.
