The worst I can see it getting realistically is "One day to hang out with one ally, then MOME or Stuffed Walrus for the other," or "Two days total to get enough FP to hang out with both of them."

While it is true that as more people join, eventually get to the point where they have them and are running missions with them, the FP needed for hanging out is going to gradually rise, that is mostly handled by the scaling number of people the ally will hang out with. When it does get to those levels, people using MOMEs and Stuffed Walruses more often for them will also allow the FP needed for hanging out to drop slightly as well.
