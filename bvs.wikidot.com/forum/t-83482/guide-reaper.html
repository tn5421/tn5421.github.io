Reaper Guide
North 23 Aug 2008 21:46

[[toc]]

General Guide

  • Starting with Step 3, you can only use Reaper Allies, Reaper Jutsu, and Bloodline Specific Jutsu.
  • You can learn Shorty's Jutsu whenever you want. You will need all of them as a Requirement for the last Quest.
  • Use the Reaper Allies and teams to help you.

Step 1: Unlock Reaper Missions

Complete the quest The Path of the Reaper.
Requirements:

  • Heck Butterfly - found on the General D-Rank Mission Save the Soul Reaper!
▼ Tips
▲ Tips
  • Most of the villages have more than one extra Heck Butterfly. You may ask if you can have one.
  • You can get one yourself from the General D-Rank Mission Save the Soul Reaper!. Here are some ways to increase your chances:
    • Use the Epic Dog Urination Technique or the WhiteEye Jutsu.
    • Use the team Let the Dog Out Bonus (Spot and Red Rover).

Step 2: Unlock Higher Reaper Missions

Complete the quest Hijinks Ensue.
Requirements:

  • TicTac - found on the Reaper Genjutsu Mission D-Level! Count the Substitute Souls.
  • Robogirl - found on the Reaper Taijutsu Mission D-Level! Go Grocery Shopping.

Step 3: Get the Sword of the Reaper

Complete the quest The Voice of the Soul.
Requirements:

  • Soul Candy
▼ Info
▲ Info
  • You lose two levels in Genjutsu, Ninjutsu and Taijutsu. It is recommended to complete this quest as soon as you can.
  • This quest can only be completed once.

Step 4: Get the Blood of the Reaper Bloodline

Complete the quest Over the Edge.
Requirements:

  • Soul Candy
  • Sword of the Reaper
  • MC Stripeypants - found at the Reaper Ninjutsu mission B-Level! Get a Deal at the Shop
▼ Info
▲ Info
  • Season 2+ players don't have to wait the three days.

Step 5: Get the Captain's Jacket

Complete the quest The Reaper Council.
Requirements:

  • Shorty
  • Strawberry

Optional: Get Timmy Lvl. 2

Timmy levels up at the Reaper Taijutsu mission B-Level! Teach A New Soul Reaper.

▼ Tips
▲ Tips
  • Recommended allies (Timmy must be in your team):
    • Tats
    • Strawberry or Shorty
  • Recommended jutsu:
    • Kido:Iron Maiden
  • Use Reaper Blood - Shikai.
  • If this is not enough then you can use a Powerful Potion.
  • It might have to complete this mission more than once for him to level up.

Step 6: Get the True Blood of the Reaper Bloodline

Complete the quest Adventures in the Other Side.
Requirements:

  • Shorty's Jutsu
    • Kido: Iron Maiden
    • Kido: Slayer
    • Kido: Metalocalypse
  • Tats
  • Hermano
  • Fletch
  • Named Sword of the Reaper
  • Blood of the Reaper
  • 50 Silver Petals
