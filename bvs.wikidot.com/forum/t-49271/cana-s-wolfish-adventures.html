A petite kunoichi stepped out from the shadows, her long brown hair smouldering as she quickly flattened the flames with her palms.
"Kami damn these stupid fireball traps!!" she exclaimed loudly, unsheathing a kunai and ridding herself of any dead hair, bringing it into a bob that ended a few centimetres below her chin. She brushed the soot from her scouser style skirt and 3/4 lengths combo, and resheathed her kunai into its holster on her back. Noticing the eccentric nin before her, she immediately entered a fighting stance, shuriken drawn as they were easiest to access.
"And just who do you think you are? Did you set these traps?!"
"Iie! I'm here to disable them! How'd you get in here anyway?" Cana retorted.
"I…um…er….I snuck in to train…my exam's in three weeks time and I wanna make sure I pass…I've already failed three times…" she added half-heartedly, itching her neck in dismay. Her eyes widened as she realized the hair on her back was gone. "ARGHRGH if I find out who set those traps they're so…." she clenched her hands in prospective vengance. Cana began to back away slowly as the brunette entered into furious ramblings, noticing the words, 'kunai', 'tree', and 'sun don't shine' which made her highly uneasy.
"HEEY!! Since I'm in the mood, Wanna spar?!"
And Cana, never wanting to shy from a fight, entered her usual fighting stance, wolfishly grinning at the prospect of combat.
"I like your spirit! Wanna be combat buddies? Oh, I'm Skip by the way…ehehehe…Who are you?"
"Well…we're in the same village and you don't know who I am?!"
"Gomen, I'm not good with names…or faces for that matter…" Skip replied sheepishly, her body relaxing from her stance.
Cana slapped a hand to her face at the girl's obvious ignorance. "I'm Cana Hatake, special jounin from the village hidden in darkness. Also known as the Nightmare Village, among other names." she growled.
"SPECIAL JOUNIN! Hwaaaah! Cool!!" Skip grinned, shaking her fists in triumph. "Finally! Someone who can train me that's not a loser!"
Cana sweatdropped and hung her head in quiet anguish. Just WHAT had she gotten herself in for?

"ehehehe I'm sorry I just dreamt that I was falling…" Skip scratched the back of her head and still muttered curses where her hairline ended, although, she argued with herself, now it would be much easier to manage and the change may attract SOMEONE'S attention. Cana looked at her quizically as she argued with herself, the girl's fragile state of sanity clearly showing. To her surprise, she stopped, fumbled in her side pack and found a tatty, palm-sized book that was so well used it didn't close correctly anymore. "Falling…Falling…" she muttered, scanning the pages with crazy eyes as a mad fan-girl might watch the target of their affections. "May be yielding to certain feelings…fear of rejection…" Skip sighed.
"Any of that even apply to you?"
Skip's expression changed to one of disappointment, and putting the faded little book away she shook her head sadly.
"aghrghgrhghgghgh!! WHAT IS THAT?!" Skip squealed, pointing to the black winged creature fluttering around her, that just happened to settle itself on her nose. She turned deathly pale. Letting out a scream of terror she ran for it. The poor little butterfly shaken by its ordeal, rested gently on the tree bark. She kept going until eventually, her lack of knowledge for her surroundings got the better of her and slamming into another being she was thrown to the ground.
"What the…" a voice asked, seeing the poor kunoichi well out of her element. Scrambling to her knees she unsheathed a kunai, shaking as she stood up but still with a look of fury in her eyes. she had been terrified, now she was lost and afraid of the unknown before her, whose face was masked by the shadows in the trees. Her instincts told her to attack, since the being's chakra level was much higher than that of her own and a swift charge may be her only hope of escape. She was therefore, greatly surprised, and relieved, to find a small black puppy licking at her ankles.
"Ehehehehehe!" she giggled softly and stroked the puppy's ears, paying careful attention to behind his ears earning her some playful nudges to continue. Picking him up she cuddled him gently and allowed him to keep lookout on her shoulder.
"I wonder who that weirdo was…they've disappeared now…Finally I'm not talking to myself!" she said to her new comrade, who barked in agreement.
"Ok Shadow..let's find Cana!"

Skip cuddled her new puppy to herself.
A melancholy smile crossed her face, it was unlikely things could remain like this for long.
Soon she would reach chunnin, and would be given more difficult tasks, and her dream of being a tai-jutsu jounin was far off.
She had to train, and keep training, until she reached her breaking point.
Advancing further as a kunoichi was her only goal in life, and very little else took hold of her mind.
Her family had trained her to work to a single mission and avoid breaking from it.
Putting the puppy down she giggled lightly as he chased his tail in mirth, only to tire and lie helplessly on the ground.
Stretching her arm, leg and back muscles, she started to run on the spot a little, motioning for her new sidekick to follow.
The puppy looked at her, somewhat confused…just what was this strange kunoichi going to do?
Bringing her hands together in front of her torso, almost in a prayer-like pose, she began to focus chakra into her bloodstream, allowing it to pulse through her muscles and provide warmth and energy.
Slowing moving into the 'ready' position, Shadow stood beside her, almost mimicking her stance.
What came next was most surprising.
A blue silouhette, with no features other than being in the shape of the kunoichi, erupted from her body, racing off into the distance.
Skip meanwhile, moved to a crouching position under the shade and security of a tree, as her eyes closed silently.
Now, she could see through the eyes of the chakra clone she had created, but not for long. Her body jolted suddenly, and she reopened her eyes as exhaustion made its way through her muscles.
"You like it Shadow? It's my own technique…the Nameless Face jutsu. No-one can tell it's me since its basically a clone with no copied features…"

"Cana-chan? Uhhhh… Did you see that clone thing? How cool was that? I wonder who uses that technique…"
