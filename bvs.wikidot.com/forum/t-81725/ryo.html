Chasers and Ryo Farming
Soren_Kanzaki 19 Jun 2009 21:45

Generally, I don't recommend you use chasers when ryo farming, unless you really want a side drop. What I mean is this:

Emo3 + Olmek is a given. None of Emo's drops are useful for anything but cash, really.

It's the third party member that makes a difference. If you use, say, Billy3, and you really want Buckets or Bombs (for Tempest, S or No S, etc.; for using/trading), then yes, go ahead and use the chasers. Same applies for Proof Reader, for obvious reasons.

However, the % drop bonus is usually not as much as the cost of the chasers. E.g., you spent 20,000 on Chasers, but only got, say, 15,000 more in drops. The Chasers are therefore a bad idea. The way to determine if that is so for you is to run a day without Chasers. Note the retail value of your sales. If you drink X Chasers, the value of your sales must be at least: Cost of Chasers / (.1 * X).
