tsundere 29 Jun 2009 08:16

Since the recode, nearly all bonuses are additive, and friend points fall into this category.

In other words:
J Diddy +400%
Tic Tac +400%
Total +800%

- Of course, realize that you are splitting the total amount between the number of friends.

Other items that affect this:
Love Love Paradise +100%

Rack On The Rocks (Juice Bar), +5% per each up to stacks up to 12 times* or +10% up to 12 times with Drunken Fist Theme activated also.

The Ninja-Mas Spirit Jutsu +100%
Running Man +400* (Girl Power Team only)

Themes:
Battle of Champions: +100%
Good Night Ninja +1250 - NON MISSION, given at end of day, NOT divided

Example: When I was running missions for The Tote, I had J-Diddy, Tic Tac, and Right, and was receiving 540.

Love Love Paradise (100%) + J-Diddy (400%) + Right (400%) = 900% per mission (where 100% is the baseline).

From there, 540 divided by 9 = the baseline of 60 per mission.

I'm probably off on something here, but I'm too tired to think about it any more

Moare 01 Jul 2009 00:12

Base friend points per Mission is $90-15*allies$ points per ally, so by only using 2 allies you get $90-15*2=60$ per ally per mission.

By using boosters like like J-Diddy and TicTac gets you $Base+J-DiddyBoost*Base+TicTacBoost*Base=100+400+400=900$%.

Calculated by using Points you get $60+60*4+60*4=60+240+240=60*9=540$ Point per mission if you only use J-Diddy and TicTac.

If you adding a third ally, you get $90-15*3=45$ points per ally before boosters which gives you $45*9=405$ Points per Ally per Mission when including TicTac and J-Diddy in your team.

Adding a Love Love Paradise: $Base+J-Diddy*Base+TicTac*Base+LoveLoveParadice$$=100+400+400+100=1000$% => $45*10=450$ Points per Ally per Mission

Tested and Confirmed

To answer your question: you gain +800%
