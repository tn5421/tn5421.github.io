Buretsu 24 Dec 2009 06:36

Okay, let's take this from the top, and make it as simple as I can:

Lycoris is not the cost you pay to start fighting the 11-Tailed.
Lycoris is the item you have to give up in order to get Eleven Tails.
It's the exact same process as giving up a Polar Star in order to get a Holy Spur, except you get Lycoris back immediately afterwards.

11-Tailed Revenge works in the same way, only you have to 'give up' Eleven Tails to get Kimono of Shadows.
