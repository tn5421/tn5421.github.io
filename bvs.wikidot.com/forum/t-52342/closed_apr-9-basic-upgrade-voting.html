Apr. 9 - Basic Upgrade Voting
BvS-Uzuki 09 Apr 2008 07:13

From here on, we're going to have two votes running at once. This thread is for upgrades that do not require any advanced resources. These upgrades are a lower priority than the advanced resource upgrades, since the resources are easier to get. We will purchase a basic upgrade whenever we have more ryo than we need for the current advanced upgrade we're planning and if we currently lack the advanced resources we'd need. This will happen more often than you think!

Normal Upgrades

  • Fortified Walls
  • Ninja Attack Squad
  • Infiltration Academy - 1 - Jupiah
  • Saboteur School
  • Genin Training School
  • Chunin Training School
  • Meditation Rooms
  • Solidarity
  • Siege Engines
  • Invasion Defense
  • Interrogators
  • Ninja Dog Kennel
  • Sand Village Trading Post - 1 - GogoluG
  • Scattered Outposts
  • Bingo Academy
  • General Store
  • Special Forces (Ninjutsu) - 1 - OmnifoxGE
  • Special Forces (Taijutsu)
  • Spy Network - 8 - Ainn, Ananvil, Bayard, Darkshard, Lear, naito, schon, Uzuki
  • Jonin Training Academy
  • Tools of the Trade

Please vote in the following format:

Vote: <Character Name> - <Upgrade Preference>
