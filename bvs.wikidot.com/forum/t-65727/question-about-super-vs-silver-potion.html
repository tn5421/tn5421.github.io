silver gives 100k daily EXP.

Now what im wondering is will the 100 extra stamina give me MORE xp (if i win ever mission) or will the silver potion give me more overall xp?

Since i could only get 50k exp per every 100 stamina.

This is a question for the Jonin Test season 2

the silver is better over all but does it even work for the test? I can only use 1. so I think ill go with the silver if it works on the test.

Gogolu 09 Jun 2008 12:10

With super potion you will have bonus 10 missions(100stamina). +1 mission from +10 stamina from Pinky from 10x1 stamina. In total 11 missions.

With theme Teacher's Pet (x2 xp) it will give you 12-14k xp per double attribute B rank mission.(Counting standard Aura of Experience/Letter of Marque, Captain's Jacket, Shortbunny and Timmy, Panda and Pinky)

It means super potion will give you opportunity for 150k xp. One and half of Silver Elixir. All this counting on smoke-bombing for double attribute missions.

Silver Elixir is on other hand sure thing, no gambling.
