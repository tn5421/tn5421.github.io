Buretsu 12 Feb 2009 07:55

Yeah, that's the equivalent to the rewards you got in the old arena.

Win 10 matches in a day and get a Power related to your current Bloodline. Legacy get Jazz Hands, Red Eye get Eye of the Tiger, and White Eye get X-Ray Specs. Respectively, these give +3 Gen/Nin/Tai Strength in the Arena.

If you're Season 2+, 10 wins also gets you Doughman, needed for Monochrome.

Win 15 matches in a day, and get a Warrior. Wisdom for Legacy, Courage for Red Eye, and Power for White Eye. Respectively, they give +1 Gen/Nin/Tai Range everywhere, and you get 5 Stamina back when you attack a Kaiju of that particular Stat.
