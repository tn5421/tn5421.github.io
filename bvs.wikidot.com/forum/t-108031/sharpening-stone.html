sebmagic 19 Mar 2009 17:57

Larry and Haro are useful once you are in the Wasteland (2nd portion) and you have access to the Kaiju Monuments, which can drop ash-covered runes that can result in valuable runes. Those missions are 10 difficulty/10 success, so not hard to accomplish.

Your best team to get drops if you have an adequate range is to use Haus, Good Boy, and Bones lvl 2, with the I Need This Jutsu taught by Good Boy.

As for the odds of getting Sharpening stones, I have three extras laying around, after doing two scythes in S1, and prepare 2 Sharp Crystal for the scythe I will do in S3. I would say it's all a matter of luck. Pray the RNG that he will be merciful with you in the future, and good luck.

For Note Page, your doing it wrong. To get them, you either use the Prize Wheel and hope for that drop there, while running the mission you are going for… Or, you use the note page you currently have, Bingo people with them (use Spot jutsu, Epic Dog Urination Technique for extra points), get 200 style points and unlock Hyuk. Do about 50 missions with Hyuk and you should have your 10 Note Pages. Good luck again.
