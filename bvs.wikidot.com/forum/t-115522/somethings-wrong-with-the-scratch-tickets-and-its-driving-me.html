You only select 3 spots from the top 3 lines. If you select more then 3 only the first 3 you clicked are counted.

The 4th place you choose from is from the bonus line at the bottom. If you have a rare super card the bonus line will have a potion spot and if you pick that spot plus 3 potions up top then you will the potion.
