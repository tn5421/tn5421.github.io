FaintIce 14 Aug 2008 02:28

Well different things lead to the factors that tell you the number of juices you can consume (like rank, season, items, etc.), i.e., your Limit and Tolerance. Your limit is the total number of Juices you can consume, while the Tolerance is how many you can take before the penalties start. With my S1 Jonin chara, I have a limit of 12 and a tolerance of 2. And from what I understand, the penalties don't take affect until the next day, but I don't know for sure as I haven't gone over my Tolerance. As of this moment, I think the only item that affects the the negative effects is the Hollow Leg (again, I'm not 100% on that).

As for the Sannin run, I take the +100stam Ramen. I can get 100k rxp easy just running A-ranks with smokebombs. And especially now with NinjaBurger, the +40app Ramen isn't really necessary, well imo it isn't.

Jadian 15 Aug 2008 04:10

Since you can get either a +5 Stamina or a +5 Appetite drink, the max stamina you could juice up is 60, while you could juice up 30 appetite/30 stamina and go with Teriyaki Ramen instead ( assuming you're legacy ).

I was wondering why your numbers were so low, since I didn't have Note Rush to help out. Figures that you were forgeting something :P Although you may be counting it too high, if you're taking XP values from when you complete the missions and aren't removing the XP bonuses applied due to your items.

As far as beating the missions, that's why you'll probably use Emo3/Stalker2/Timmy2 along with Shikai to max out your strength? That's +7 strength/+3 range/+1 success on top of (since you mentioned using nin for your run) Ninja Dog, Giant Fan, and other items that boost your stats. You'll be good for most missions.

The downtime penalty is stamina, and -levels for the day if the penalty is quite large.
