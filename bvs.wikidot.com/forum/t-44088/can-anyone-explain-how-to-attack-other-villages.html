Re: Can anyone explain how to attack other villages?
Anonymous (206.81.147.x) 15 Mar 2008 02:34

It takes 50 Stamina to attack.

Generally, most times one attacks, you'll see difficulty and successes in the low teens, but once enhancements and such go into place, you can run into low 20s.

Not that much of a problem at the higher levels. There are Summons and Items that boost your range and also Ashen War Paint that is +10 to range. With the right equipment, often from Monsters, hitting difficulties in the mid 30s is not unheard of.

Re: Can anyone explain how to attack other villages?
Ruinic 23 Mar 2008 03:05

The max difficulty with all the defence upgrades seems to be around 37. Anything higher than that is due to peacetime. It's impossible for any ninja to achieve 76 successes. In fact, any number of successes above 15 should be considered impossible since that amount of peacetime will also give a very high difficulty.
Invasion Defence buffs difficulty by 20. Only Legacy nin can use the summon that increases range in attacks.
