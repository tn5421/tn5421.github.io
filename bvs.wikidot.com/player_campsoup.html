Character Description

History

campsoup was a lone human with the soul of a cat trapped in him. His mother died while giving birth to him. His father is unknown, even to him. Because of his appearances, the village he was born in shunned him because they thought he had demon blood. The Hokage of the village saw hints of the potential in him, and ordered the villagers to accept him. He was passed from home to home to stay in for a month or two but then was kicked out because the villagers thought he brought bad luck to anyone that was near him and blamed him for anything that went wrong, even if it was extremely unlikely that he was involved. By the time it came to the Chunnin Exams, campsoup was staying with Hokage because none of the other villagers would let him into their homes. Just before the end of the Chunnin Exams, the Hokage was found dead in bed riddled with kunkais and no signs of a fight or forced entry. The villagers quickly suspected that campsoup was involved, but no one openly blamed him because there was no evidences against him and to try to honor the Hokage's respect for campsoup.

campsoup passed the Exams with flying colors and was put in a team with the two other ninjas that where willing to work with him and had previously shown signs of becoming a powerful team. When they got their first mission outside of the village, campsoup was excited but his two partners was depressed and very nervous. campsoup tried asking them a couple of times what was wrong, but each time, they refused to look him in the eye and couldn't answer him. When they got out of sight of the village walls, one of campsoup's partners mumbled his name. When campsoup turned around, the partner cast Nehan Sh≈çja no Jutsu (Temple of Nirvana Technique).

When campsoup woke up, he found himself on a raft floating in the middle of the sea a few miles off shore with just his clothes, a container of food pills, and a note. They took all of his equipment and weapons, and even his headband! The note read, "We are sorry we had to do this to you but the orders came from the new Hokage. We where even told we couldn't leave you with those food pills. We hope you could forgive us as we didn't have any choice in the matter." It was signed by both of his partners.

When campsoup made it to shore, he wondered from town to town trying to find a home but none would accept him. He eventually found himself in Volcano Village which accepted him and trained him to be a ninja that the Village is proud to have!
