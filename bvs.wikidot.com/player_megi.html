History

You got a Warning!
Really Bad History!
(Total: 1)

Megi woke up one stereotypical day to find himself in front of a stereotypical Numbered Overly Triangular Huge Integrated Neverending Gorge. Thinking "What the heck" he jumped in. Several hours later he hit the bottom. (Wait…what?) Megi stood up, somehow unharmed by the insane drop. Suddenly he noticed something missing. He checked his wallet, and some of his money was gone! "Stupid gorge" Megi thought, as he reflected upon his lost riches. Suddenly, something landed on Megi. "OW." Megi said as he got up. When he looked at what had hit him, it was Flipper, the "Talking" Dolphin! Megi looked up for his other allies, but couldn't see any of them, so he asked Flipper;
"Where is everybody?"
Flipper responded with a rather un-dolphinlike "BARK." Megi, apparently having taken some head trauma from the fall after all, heard;
"You fell into a gorge! Flipper came to help you! No one else came though…"
Megi decided to give up on this conversation and decided to just carry on as usual, as he began to suspect more things were going wrong…starting with the fact that dolphins were talking to him.
