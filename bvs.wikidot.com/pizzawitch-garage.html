Get Delivery Missions

This page is where you select and begin delivery missions.

The Discern stats represents the percentage chance you have to avoid the penalties of the random events.
You fail the Delivery if any of the Drivetrain, Durability, or Deliciousness drops to zero.
You may only go on a set number of delivery missions in a day. This number can be increased by items and by the Awesome Ability Initial Drifter.

There are two types of Delivery Missions:

  • Regular deliveries reward you with:
    • Tips: Base_Tip * Deliciousness / 100.
      • Minimum Deliciousness used for the tip reward is 50.
      • Maximum Deliciousness used for the tip reward is 200.
    • Ryo Coins: You have a chance of winning a Ryo Coin if you finish with Deliciousness over 100.
      • The probability to win is (Deliciousness - 100) %.
  • Special Deliveries reward you with progress through the PizzaWitch storyline.

Equip Ride

On this page, you may select and add Parts (Equipment cards) and/or Passengers to your ride. Equipment cards give their stated bonus throughout an entire delivery mission. The Burn Chance on each Equipment card indicates the probability that it will disappear permanently at the end of a ride. Each ride has a limited amount of Equipment slots. Also, many Equipment cards subtract from your ride's Durability.
See View Collections for card lists.
Equipment cards are only used in PizzaWitch deliveries; they are not items.

Load D-Abilities

Here is where you load Ability cards onto your ride. D-Abilities give temporary bonuses during a delivery missions and may be used (or not used) whenever you choose. Each card that you have loaded can be used at most once during a ride. The Burn Chance listed on each Ability card indicates the probability that the card will disappear permanently when it is used. Each ride has a limited number of D-Ability slots. Also, many Ability cards subtract from your ride's Drivetrain when they are used.
See View Collections for card lists.
Ability cards are only used in PizzaWitch deliveries; they are not items.

Trade / Sell Cards

Here is where you can trade and/or sell your cards. You can sell any card for 15 tips. If you choose to trade a card you will either get a) a card of the same rarity out of the same set, b) a card of one rarity level lower out of the same set or, c) 10 tips. Each of these has an equal chance of happening.

Example 1: You want to trade a Super-Rare card from the set Easy Street. You have a 33.3% chance of getting another Super-Rare card from easy street, a 33.3% chance of getting a Rare card from Easy Street, and a 33.3% chance of getting 10 tips.
Example 2: You want to trade a Common card from the Easy Street. Since there is no rarity level below Common, you have a 50% chance of getting another Common from Easy Street and a 50% chance of getting 10 tips.

Train a Stat

There are three trainable stats (the DDDs): Drift, Drive, Destruction. Each starts at a base value of 2 and can be trained for an increasing amount of tips. You may train your stat to a number higher than the maximum your ride allows, but you will not gain any benefit past your ride's limit. There is no known cap.

3 -> 4 4,000 tips
4 -> 5 8,000 tips

7 -> 8 64,000 tips

11 -> 12 1,024,000 tips
n -> (n+1) 1000*2(n-1) tips

Make Pizzas

This page is a mini-crafting screen for the creation of pizzas.
Ingredients come from appraising PizzaWitch Ally Drops.
Pizzas are unlocked when you have the ingredients to make them.
The chance to make 2 pizzas is Chance / 2 (bonuses included in Chance).
The chance to make 3 pizzas is Chance / 4 (bonuses included in Chance).

Work in the Back

Work a shift on the other Riders' Rides for a cut of their Tips - and Coins!

The amount of Tips (and Coin types) you receive per shift is based on the level of Shift you undertake. Once you beat a Delivery at any Difficulty 20 times4, you may work a Shift at that Difficulty.

  • The bonus you have to Tips applies to shifts too.
  • Chance to get Ryo Coin (Initial Drifter bonus applies):
    • Base: 10%
    • Bribe: 25%
    • Mega Bribe: 40%
