Character Description

Buffy's appearance varies. Because of the power she gained by completing The Impossible Mission, she has the ability to change form at will. Her current state is not yet known.

History

Buffy, born a Slayer, had been fighting for most of her life. Demon after demon, vampire after vampire, she defeated for over seven years. She has gained many items from her battles. When she defeated a gang, know simply as the Vampires, she gained the Vampire Cloak, which has helped her immensely in her many battles. Buffy fought many types of creatures and had many interesting experiences. From actually dying, losing her memory, even spontaneously bursting into song she'd done it all. But after seven years, she'd grown tired of that lifestyle. One day, she heard of a place where people train as ninja, and become stronger, by attempting missions given to them, fighting monsters, and gaining special items and themes. She goes to the first village she sees, the Obvious Village, right there in the open. After being a loyal villager and earning the trust of her Kage, she is left to lead the village in his place, as he has other business to attend to.

Finally finishing The Impossible Mission, she leaves the ruins of Obvious behind and sets out to find a new goal. With her new Immortal Realm, Noir, in place, she is steadily working hard to become the best.
