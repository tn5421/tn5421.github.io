Jutsu (Untabbed)

Jutsu are the special abilities that a ninja utilizes in combat. These abilities either use consumables or they consume chakra. In most cases, jutsu are utilized to increase chances for success, though in some cases they have other special purposes. Jutsu are primarily learned from allies, but can also be learned at a higher XP cost from village trainers. The RedEye bloodline ability also provides access to new jutsu.

Learning a jutsu costs XP. XP is short for "Experience Points." You gain a certain number of XP for each mission you go on. Generally, you will get more XP for successfully completing a mission than if you fail it (though you will still receive some for failure). In addition, you gain more XP for completing harder missions than you do for completing easier ones. After XP is gained, you spend that XP learning a jutsu (reducing your total XP). XP does not affect your level in any way, it is only used for purchasing jutsu.
