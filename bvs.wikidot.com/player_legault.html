Character Description

Legault is a tall male shinobi, 19 years old, with shoulder long black hair and purplish-white eyes. When he's on missions, he likes to wear K-dog's headband, as a memory of the night in which he beat him while playing poker, but more than the memory, the reminder of the one situation in day to day life in which WhiteEye beats RedEye. He also wears the soul gloves he found on a random evening, while running his missions some time ago now, after helping some girl. Sides from that he wears a black vest that shows his athlete complexity and the tight ninja pants. Whenever he goes to the other side in missions, he always puts the long cloak the grim reaper uses, just for the sake of the joke. Whenever he goes to the wasteland, he wears the desert robesWhenever he's hanging out with his friends, or playing with trainers and fellow ninja in the party house, goes to the ramen shop on a date or is just hanging out on the lounge of the village HQ, he can be found playing his guitar if he's nothing to do, or flirting with some nice kunoichis… as long as these aren't of the super strong type. Here he can be found with more former trousers and always wearing THE jacket. Y'know the black leather jacket every bad boy uses. His motto is "always go past the limit".

History

Early Years

Legault's ninja carreer started… well pretty badly. When he was 6, he noticed that his eyes were different from the other kids. So he started playing with them at home, and found that he had the ability of the WhiteEyes. However his parents didn't want him to be a ninja, because they were overprotective. But he always managed to sneak past the villa's guards and find a glade where to train.

One day he was returning home from the glade, and he sensed a thief trying to steal an old lady's mask. He went after the thief, but the theif was too much for poor little Legault, aged 7. However he gave quite a fight showing some remote self-taught mastery of the White Eye. Before the thief managed to kill him, a swordsman came from behind and paralyzed him using a jutsu. This swordsman would become a very important person for Legault, though at that time, he didn't know. All he knew was that he saved him, and that he had offered himself to train him. For a year, the swordsman trained Legault to get the best out of his WhiteEye. He learned to recognize his environment around him and to make the best of it. He also went through the 50 meter limit of his WhiteEye, little by little, until it reached 60 meters.

One day, he was returning to his villa, he found it under attack. A band of ninjas was attacking his home. Using his WhiteEye he managed to get to the front of his house, and he realized that it was a coordinated attack. His parents were holding back the back and front entrance, and the guards had the sides covered. However no one was protecting the roof. He quickly got there to intercept the same thief that had wanted to kill him a year ago. This time he was no match for Legault. However, ninjas were still swarming towards the manor. Legault went down to help to the best of his ability, however they were too many. Luckly, a ninja squad arrived and helped them hold them off. The team leader talked to Legault's parents about his potential and convinced them. This is how Legault met Flipper, and how his ninja life started.

Genin
Once Legault became a genin, he rushed into missions. Maybe too many missions. Since he sensed his surroundings everytime he could, he would become exhausted rapidly, but he still kept going. However, this had a bright side. He piled up a load of stuff from his missions, and he happened to find quiet glades and other places where he could meditate. He also found lots of valuable allies that would help him in the future, such as Pinky and Billy.

Since most of the missions Legault runned were domestic, he got bored easily. His goal was to achieve chuunin, so he could go in more difficult missions. But this goal was far away from him, and a lot of training was required. So he trained. And he trained until his ninjutsu, genjutsu and taijutsu skills were above of those of all other genin. On his training, using the jutsu learned from Red Rover, he found some strange butterflies. To learn more about those butterfilies, Legault visited the Wiki village, a stronghold of information, where many competent ninjas were constantly supplying the wiki. On the Wiki's wiki, he learned that those butterflies were the heck and swallowtail butterflies, and that they were beings out of this world. However he never payed much attention to them, just thanked the fact that they would give him extra energy to run around.

Chuunin
After his training, Legault had already found all the allies he could. He had trained his skills to the maximum (actually to the minimum required for the chuunin exam) and he knew that he could do it. So he presented himself for the chuunin exam. He was really excited. He entered the exam room and prepared to take the first part of the exam. When he sat down and saw the paper he thought there would be no problem. The examinator announced the rules, and when he said no jutsus allowed… Legault panicked. The exam was so traumatizing that he still remembers the questions on detail. The first one was something about ninjas he didn't understand. The second was a math problem on a shuriken (seriously, how is this useful? Just aim and throw). The third question was a history question (again, how is this useful?). The fourth question were strange symbols (okay, I don't even speak this language. Maybe if I copy this symbol here, use this other one there…). The fifth question was about the seven uses of toes in assasination (anything can be a lethal weapon, but this is ridiculous). The sixth question was about feeding a dog (ok, who is the very smart person who makes these questions?). The seventh question was blank (again, who makes this?). The eighth question was about the uses of sexy jutsu on a hot date (how do THEY even know that jutsu…). The ninth question was about how quickly would an enemy burn with a certain jutsu (I don't care, he doesn't move, he's burning in pain, that's enough for me). The tenth question was just "Name:".
