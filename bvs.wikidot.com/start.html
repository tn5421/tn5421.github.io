Welcome to the Billy Vs. SNAKEMAN Wiki!


What is "Billy Vs. SNAKEMAN"?

Billy Vs. SNAKEMAN (often called BvS) is a browser based RPG very loosely based on the popular anime series Naruto. You have the ability to create your own ninja with the bloodline of your choice, go on missions, advance in rank, advance your skills in genjutsu, ninjutsu, taijutsu, and doujutsu arts, and gather allies along the way. While playing the game can be simple enough, it has reached a point where there is literally so many things that a character can do or attain that a Wiki was required!
