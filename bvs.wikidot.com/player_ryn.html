Character Description

Ryn is a male shinobi in his late twenties and he's about 6 feet 2 inches tall. Ryn has a medium build and his hair is a very spiky, vibrant blond. Ryn's normal wardrobe is his SWATBU uniform which complements him. His eyes are an icy blue but in the case he uses his WhiteEye bloodline they turn a gray-white. Ryn has very distinctive red tattoo on the side of his arm. Always by Ryn's side is his trusty Sword of the Reaper, Zenhoshi. There is also Ryn's famous SWATBU mask attached to his hip. The mask is in the shape of a eagles' head and distinguishes his loyalty to the almighty Bird Village. Ryn is also the Second Eaglekage of the Bird Village.

History

A Powerful Beginning

"Where am I?" Ryn said aloud as he woke up. The last thing he could remember was a big explosion and his body gotten flung into the sky. He looked around at his surroundings and saw a village a ways off. As Ryn walked towards the village, he noticed many birds flying in the sky overhead. As he approached the village gates, Ryn noticed that there was an eagle carved into the arc above him. He gently pushed the gates upon. "Is anyone there?" he shouted at the top of his lungs. Hearing no response, Ryn continued to wandering the village. Eventually, he came upon a building with the sign 'Kage' on it. Ryn entered the building and quickly found the main room but to his surprise there was a figure there. The figure turned around and faced him, "Welcome to the Bird Village!" the figure said, as Ryn identified the figure to be a girl. "What's you name?" she asked. "My name's Ryn" he replied. "Well Ryn, you are now officially the Second Eaglekage of the Bird Village!" As she started to walk out of the room. "Wait, what's your name?" Ryn asked. "My name's MisaTange." She said, as she walked out of the room with a smile on her face. Ryn was 13.

In the days soon to follow, Ryn easily acheived the rank of Genin. After achieving the rank of Genin, Ryn quickly became friends with Pinky, an invaluable ally that would help him on his journey as a ninja.
