Missions (Untabbed)



Missions are the most common way to level up your character. Missions vary in difficulty, with the easiest being D-Rank missions and the hardest being S-Rank Missions. There are also Special Missions, which require a condition to be met before they can be unlocked. A mission can increase your jutsu XP, your attributes and earn your character some Ryo. Rarely, they can also provide you with allies and items.

Each mission has a particular condition for success. In most cases it requires that you attain a certain number of successes at a particular difficulty rating. For more information, check the attributes article.

To find out what rank you need to be to access each mission rank go here (Player Rank)

A Rare Mission can only be obtained by taking General Missions.

Quests cost 10 Stamina per part.
Failed Quests allow you to start at the last part you were at.
