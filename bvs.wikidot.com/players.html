Players

There are literally thousands of people playing Billy Vs. SNAKEMAN on a daily basis. Some of them have put an extraordinary amount of effort into the back story of their character for roleplaying purposes. This Wiki allows for players to create a Wiki page specific to their own character.

Getting My Own Page

Creating your own Wiki page for your character is easy! Just type your character's name into the box below and hit "Create My Page!" You'll be delivered to your own custom page which you can edit with your character's information. Only you (and the administrators) can edit this page, unlike the rest of this Wiki, so you don't have to worry about someone libelling your good name! Furthermore, you don't need a full site membership to create your character's page, just register a Wikidot account, and off you go!

Your character's name?

Help!

If you have any questions, hop on over to the forum and feel free to ask away! If you feel that someone has taken your character's name incorrectly, please put a post in the forum, and the administrators will handle this situation as quickly as possible.

Notice

Pages that have been created but have done minimal to help explain the character (i.e. they create the template, their portrait and signature and not much more than that) may be tagged for deletion. If 7 days after the tag has been put in place, the page remains without an update, it will be deleted. Empty player pages don't really help anyone, and they end up taking space that may be better used.
