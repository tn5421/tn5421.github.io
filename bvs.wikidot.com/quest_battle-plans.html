Battle Manual

▼ Manual
▲ Got it

Your Objective is to get to the end of a sequence of Zones. You can get through a Zone in one of two ways:

1. Destroying the Tower
2. Bypassing the Tower

If you Bypass a Tower, you are penalized for the rest of this battle. You cannot Bypass the last tower.

Ninjas have Threat - As long as there is any possible way out of a situation, they survive unscathed. Enemies add Threat, and if you get too much, you lose. Threat lowers when you change Zones.

Kaiju have HP, which goes down when they take damage. They have a lot of HP, but it doesn't refresh as easily.

To stay alive, you require Spiral Energy - it is expended as you fight, or when you switch forms. If it runs out, you automatically fail.

Ninja can attack one type of enemy a turn - every hit they land is lethal. Kaiju cannot target individual enemies when attacking, but crush them randomly en masse based on the enemy HP.

Kaiju can also charge and fire a beam - if the beam is strong enough to overwhelm all enemy HP in that area, the area is wiped clean. This is one of the only ways to attack an area outside of your own.

Moving backwards is always allowed. Moving forwards and Sabotaging can be blocked by minions.

To defeat the Tower, you need to get close enough to it to either prepare Sabotage as a Ninja, or Attack it as a Kaiju.

If you are in Tune with an enemy (the correct Chakra in your Tune matches theirs), it does less damage to you and has a more difficult time blocking your actions. Enemies have only one Chakra type you need to match.

AP is gained as a ratio between Rai and Kai. the amount of time spent in each form during the battle determines the ratio. Each Area has a hard cap as to how high you can level a stat - should you cap out in one stat, you will be unable to get more AP in that stat until you defeat a boss.

Awesome Abilities that add to Levels do not affect Raijutsu and Kaijutsu - they are borne from the Awesome itself, so it doesn't futher affect it.

Strategy

For advice, see the HQ Guide.

HQ Musou Skills

  • Skills may be increased up to Level 3. Spiral Skills can use either Rai or Kai points.
  • Lowering a Skill causes you to lose 25% of your Raijutsu and Kaijutsu AP.
  • Ninja Skills - Raijutsu
  • Kaiju Skills - Kaijutsu
  • Spiral Skills - Raijutsu or Kaijutsu
  • Contingency (+10/30/50% Threat Max)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Flush (halve current Threat - Ninja Only)
  • Fleetness (10%/30%/50% Additional Dodge Chance)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Confront (move next to the Tower - Ninja only)
  • Pinpointing (+10/30/50% Sabo Damage)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Timestop (Enemy time does not pass for one turn)
  • Lethality (+10/30/50% Ninja Attack Count)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Blowback (knock all Minions out of your location)
  • Plating (+25/50/100% Kaiju HP)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Invincibility (take no damage from attacks next turn)
  • Stompers (+10/30/50% Stomp Armor)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Vacuum (pull all Minions to your location)
  • Beam Focus (+50/100/150% Beam Power)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Twinbeam (Beams get a second chance to hit: Passive Skill)
  • Strength (+25/50/100% Kaiju Attack Power)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Devour (Destroy Minions nearby, gaining their HP - Kaiju Only)
  • Charge (2/3/5 Max Charge Level)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Turbo (Charging skips Level 1: Passive Skill)
  • Efficiency (-1/2/3 Kaiju Spiral Burn)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Metamorph (50% chance actions cost -1 Spiral: Passive Skill)
  • Limits (+50/100/150% Starting Spiral, unused extra returned if victorious)
    • Bonus if Lvl. 3: Roll Out (Transform and move one area closer)
