Waxing
BillyClub » Waxing

BC Exclusive!
Share in 11's Retail woes by doing a job for Stripey!

How To Play

Waxing the Store
For some unknown reason, you let Stripey talk you into rewaxing the Retail store. Ugh.

You have from 6:00 PM Sunday (when the store closes) to 11:00 AM Tuesday (when it opens back up - Mondays are the store's day off) to do the following:

  • Pull up the old wax (Strip the floor - once it's wet, then scrub out the old wax)
  • Seal the floor once it is dry (optional?)
  • Start Waxing
  • Once you think a coat is good enough, Let it Dry and start again!
  • Get as many Wax coats down as you can, but be sure it's dry by open!

You can Rush if you want to get more done, but things might not go as planned..

You have a Helper to, well, help you. They are super competent, but sick of having to do this dumb job- if they agree to help you, it is like you Rushed without the downsides. (That's a big "if") Helping makes them grumpy. If they are too grumpy, watch out!

Find Endings to get permanent Waxing abilities, and get yourself some ThankYou Coins! Play once a day for free! Thanks for supporting BvS!

GRUMPY ENDING

You get so frustated with this stupid task that you aren't even getting anything for that you stomp off, leaving a trail of waxy footprints into the night.

HUNGRY ENDING

You get so snacky that, looking around frantically for something to nom, you see a bucket of delicious, delicious wax ice cream, and you can't stop yourself.. (no joke, commercial floor finish smells like vanilla soft-serve. It is creepy. Don't eat wax, folks. -11)

SLEEPY ENDING

So, so tired, you find a warm, sticky bed to lie in, and snuggle in as it envelops you..

WAXY ENDING

Struggling, you are powerless as the wax covers you completely. That night, as you are helpless within, the shell begins to move on its own..

LATE ENDING

Jolting out of bed, you realize the nightmare is real - you slept though your alarm, and forgot to open the store!

BELOW ENDING

You admire the wonderful sheen, but then from beneath, an unworldly moan - your wax has drawn up an ancient terror, and with an incomplete seal, there is nothing to stop it from feeding on the world above!

WET ENDING

The doors burst open, and customers stream in - plowing into and consumed by the still-wet floor. Thrashing, they pile up, as more stream in, unaware of their impending doom. The pile grows and grows..

BAD ENDING

You finish up, but ugh, the floor doesn't look very good at all. Why'd you even bother?

OKAY ENDING

You finish up. Not bad, you guess.

SLACKER ENDING

You lose track of the number of sandwiches you've eaten and games you played. Weren't you supposed to be doing something? Your helper shrugs. Oh well!

GLOW ENDING

The floor is perfect, radiant and strong. The customers are in awe, and you are hailed as a Hero across the land.
