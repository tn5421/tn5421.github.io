Character Description

alastor is a ninja that walk the plains between reality and fantasy, he wears an armor that conceals his body and seals his power. the was baptisedin blood and war, knowing how to fight at a young age, he has suffered many scars. He bears one tattoo over his chest of a dragon encircling a scorpian, behind then a banner with a sole word, Denise. He has a muscular build, and he carries with him a blade, made of a carcoal metal, made indestructable by forgeing within it a rune that turns and attack on the blade into energy which is then fed to the blade let it repair itself and to make it more durable. He can gather his energies into his hand in a swirl of power. His chakra's color is a unique purple and blood red. He also has a tattered cape, that bllows in the wind behind him.

History

Here lies the forgotten warrior, thought dead by his friends and abondoned by his family. His name lost in the eternal sands of time. For centuries he was a mercenary, fighting good and evil alike. He knew no fear, no pain, but he remembered his emotions. Confused after all his wandering, he stubbled into a sacred land when you are niether dead or alive. Here he met the grand executioner, Death. Death held no sympathy for this lost soul, and was about to strike him down. In an epic battle that went on for weeks, finally the warrior gave in. His body tired and soul worn, he could no longer fight. But, Death had grown a certain respect for the warrior, being able to fight him on an even level for so long and spared him from dying by his blade. Instead the warrior was throne into a pit.

He stayed in that pit for days, first five, then 10, then a month past, and then a year, countless time past in the pit and the warrior lost track of time, until finally another was thown into the pit with him. This 2nd person looked to be a foolhardy young fighter. He had exclaimed that he was the best warrior of all time, telling the warrior of all the people he had killed and the weakness of his victims. His brashness angered the warrior, for it was in his code that unless it was a paid mission, you never raised your blade, outside of battles of honor. He ask the foolish fighter to quiet himself.
"Eh, boy. Shut your mouth and hold your tongue, you know nothing of being a true blademaster."
"What did you say old man? I know that there is no one that can strike me down, and thats all I need to know."
"You are too damn arrogant boy, I know slaves that could kill you where you stand, you have a long way to go before you are at the level you claim to be at."
"Shut up, your senile. How would you know? You've probably spent your entire life here."
"Well if your so sure of that boy come, show me your strength."
"Thats it, I'm gonna cut you into a million pieces and then turn those pieces into dust an—"
The boy never finished his sentence as the blade of the warrior was at his thought "You talk to much, its annoying. Now even drop to your knees and sumbit of I kill you before you can raise that piece of plastic you call a sword."
The kid, now scared at the prowless of the warrior, dropped to his knees and begged for forgiveness.
"Shut up already, just stay out of my way and let me spend some time here in quiet, and I'll let you live."
