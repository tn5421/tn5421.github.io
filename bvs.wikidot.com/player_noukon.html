Character Description

A 5'9 Tall man that wears Black clothes, With red and black stripes on his shirt… Strangely, he wants to base his jutsu attacks on blood, He resides in Otakou village as a Jonin.

Of Course, His creator and possible (killer -cough-), is about the same. 5'9, black clothes, likes the idea of blood jutsu… Whatever.
