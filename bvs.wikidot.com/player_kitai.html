Character Description

Kitai is a long lost descendant of the great Kaguya Clan. He has medium length white hair. Across his left eye is a scar given to him by his brother when they were kids. He has an average build with a few extra pounds around the center. He wears a open blue kimono with a wire mesh undershirt. Kitai wears a pair of knee length baggy khaki's with mesh wire leggings. For shoes he sports a pair of combat boots specifically designed for his clan. Otherwise he's a semi toned pale skinned man.

History

Grew up with brother Shikyo. Later they found out they were descendants of the Kaguya clan. Around age 12 the brothers went on separate paths to help gather materials in hopes of reviving their once great clan. After 10 years the brothers have come together again to create the Marrow Village, the village hidden in the Bones.
