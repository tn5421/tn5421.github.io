Prologue

Your village is being raided! You ready yourself to protect it, as always, but this seems.. different. Something's wrong. You see a shadowy hooded figure on the rooftops point, as enemy ninjas swarm over the village. "All but this lone defender are disabled by my great Genjutsu. Destroy him, and the village is ours. Forget their resources. Kill them all."

Leap to the Attack! >

Part 1

Ninjutsu
Hordes of Enemies
Dozens of ninja swarm over the walls, and you alone must face them!
Difficulty: 8 Successes: 6
Ability #2: Taijutsu Difficulty: 5 Successes: 4

Success:

You walk through them as a reaper in a field of wheat.

Continue Quest >

Part 2

Taijutsu
Rain of Death
The Ninja fall back, and a hail of arrows - one thousand of them - fall onto your village like a driving rain. How will you knock all of them out of the air?
Difficulty: 15 Successes: 15

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Thousand Flying Water Needles of Death
  • Eight Trigrams Whirlwind
  • Twin Rising Dragons

Automatic win!

Automatic win:

Your Jutsu plucks them all from the air, saving your village!

Success:

Amazingly, you and your team defend the village from the deadly rain.

Continue Quest >

Part 3

Taijutsu
Breakthrough
The walls go down at a number of places! How will you cover them all at once?
Difficulty: 19 Successes: 9

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Clone Jutsu
  • Multiple Shadow Clone Jutsu
  • Shadow Clone Jutsu

Automatic win!

Automatic win:

You split into multiple forms, and hold the wall!

Success:

You manage to rush from opening to opening, defeating the horde.

Continue Quest >

Part 4

Genjutsu
Ninja at the Wheel
An enemy ninja stands poised to throw open the main gate! If you move an inch, they'll throw the lever - take over their mind with a Jutsu!
Difficulty: 99 Successes: 99

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Mind Body Switch Technique
  • Shadow Imitation Technique

Automatic win!

Failure:

They shake off your attempt, and unleash the wave of enemies! What Jutsu would work here?

Automatic win:

You invade their mind, and keep the gate safe!

Continue Quest >

Part 5

Ninjutsu
Suicide Charge
A horde of ninja, carrying deadly explosives, rush your position! Only a huge fireball could set them all off before they get to you!
Difficulty: 99 Successes: 99

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Fire Style: Fireball Jutsu
  • Fire Style: Dragon Flame Jutsu

Automatic win!

Failure:

They blow up half the village - do you have a Jutsu that could work here?

Automatic win:

You light all of their explosives at range, and take out the entire platoon!

Continue Quest >

Part 6

A Powerful Team

You see an opening to get to their leader, but only a full team of Jonin can get you there!

Rush Them (Requirement)
You need 3 of the following allies in your team:

  • Annie (or Annie Lvl. 2)
  • Bruce Sr. (or Bruce Sr. Lvl. 2)
  • Haus
  • J-Diddy
  • K-Dog (or K-Dog Lvl. 2)
  • K.Y. (or K.Y. Lvl. 2)
  • Rover's Mom
  • Smokey the Bear (or Smokey the Bear Lvl. 2)
  • Triple H (or Triple H Lvl. 2)
  • The Rack

Failure:

You'll need a more powerful team than that! Keep trying different Allies until you win!

If you have 1 or 2 of the required allies on your team, they are listed.
Success:

Your teammates hold back the hordes as you face their leader!

Continue Quest >

Part 7

Tri-Blooded

A strange forcefield has been erected, holding back all blood that the evil Boss has encountered! It has been made by Olmek, Lord of the Hidden Temple! You are better than that!

I Have 3 or More Bloodlines > (Requirement)

Failure:

You bounce off uselessly.

Success:

You break through, ready for action!

Continue Quest >

Part 8

Experienced

Only someone who has gone through a veritable warzone today could stomach this carnage!

I Have 300,000 or more Daily XP So Far Today > (Requirement)

Success:

You withstand the horrors and press on!

Continue Quest >

Part 9

The Power of Love

There's only one Team that can succeed here!

I have the Crazy Love Triangle Team with me (or higher levels of the Allies that make it) > (Requirement)

Success:

You four stand face-to-face with their leader!

Continue Quest >

  • Legacy of the Hero

Ninjutsu
The Boss
You find yourself face-to-face with their evil leader. He smirks at you, and pulls off his hooded cloak, revealing himself to be.. Touchy! "Finally, to wipe out the last remnants of this village. 'Kaleidoscope Eye'!"
Difficulty: 14 Successes: 13

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Flock of Seagulls
  • The Shocker
  • RedEye

Automatic win!

Automatic win:

"KALEIDO-" "KALEIDOSCOPE EYE!" You reveal your RedEye, ripping his technique from his mind, and turning it against him! "Another of my clan still lives?" He escapes into the darkness.

Success:

You soundly defeat him, and he vanishes into the night - your village is safe once again!

Taijutsu
The Boss
You find yourself face-to-face with their evil leader. He smirks at you, and pulls off his hooded cloak, revealing himself to be.. SNAKEMAN! He waves his hands, and an unending stream of snakes overcomes you!
Difficulty: 10 Successes: 2

Success:

There is a bright yellow flash, and a blond, spiky-haired ninja appears, quickly besting SNAKEMAN, who scurries away.

Without turning around, he drops a Sealed Kunai at his feet, says "if you ever need me, call me," and disappears!

Taijutsu
The Boss
You find yourself face-to-face with their evil leader. he smirks at you, and pulls off his hooded cloak, revealing himself to be.. Ol' Whitey! He seems to be under mental control, and leaps to attack you!
Difficulty: 15 Successes: 7

Success:

You soundly defeat him, and he vanishes into the night - your village is safe once again!

Perfect Jutsu Application!

  • Eight Trigrams One Million Palms
  • Eight Trigrams Whirlwind

Automatic win!

You deflect his attack with the one thing that can stop it - a Bloodline Trait! You knock him to the ground, and in so doing, breaking whatever hold is on him. He stands at your side, and helps you finish off the rest of the ninjas! "I never thought one not of the main branch would be so strong! We'll get to the bottom of this - thank you."

Also, your Legacy Ability has increased!
