UNDER CONSTRUCTION

Considered to be weird, Aisu isn't like other shinobi. Most ninja tend to wear flack jackets and other useful clothing, though there are some that just sport a unique outfit. Aisu sports a unique outfit alright. It, however, is unique even amongst the other unique outfits. This is mainly because the material and style is totally different.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

Not much is known of Aisu's past. He was born from a clan of Cooking-nin of unknown origin. However, for unknown reasons, Aisu left the clan (some speculate it was merely out of boredom). He would later meet a Chuunin from Rasta Village name Lelouch. After quickly becoming friends when Aisu personally helped Lelouch on a mission, he was persuaded by Lelouch to come back to Rasta with him. Eventually Aisu was accepted as a Rasta ninja, beginning his career as a "Real-nin", and that as they, is that.
Not long after joining Rasta, Aisu would discover a number of hidden talents he had. Most of the time these manifested during critical moments on a mission. but his first ability, he would later find out, was within him since birth: the RedEye bloodline. This manifested at a rather inopportune time. While on a mission, during a fight with an enemy nin, Aisu suddenly got a flashback to his childhood. He had poked himself in one eye, bleeding all over! After this memory passed, he found himself about to be killed and his RedEyes activated, allowing Aisu to overcome his opponent.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

A while after obtaining his RedEyes, Lelouch would tell Aisu of a few uncommon items in the village's main Storehouse that would grant him the chance to gain Other abilities. Naturally, he had been skeptical about these "Other abilities." However, after Lelouch demonstrated the power he himself had acquired, Aisu was, suffice to say, now interested. So it would come to be that these items would lead Aisu to the rescue of some weird, short girl (who would later become an ally). In gratitude, Shorty (his name for her since she was kinda rude) bestowed upon him the Substitute Soul Reaper Badge, effectively granting him permission to cruise the Other world. Aisu would make many more allies in his Other Side quests, as well as gaining more techniques.
Shortly after beginning his Other Side missions, Aisu found that he was less physically able compared to his Home World. An investigation of this would lead him to the discovery of the Soul Candy, thus eliminating the Other Side's drain on his energy. Unbeknownst to Aisu, however, he had unlocked the path to the most powerful weapon at his disposal: Absolute Zero. During missions after obtaining the Soul Candy, Aisu started to hear voices—nay, a single voice calling to him…
