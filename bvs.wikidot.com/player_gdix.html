Character Description

GoldDragon IX (GDIX) is an average build, 1,80 m high male ninja, 23 years of age. He wears a broad metal bracelet on his left arm instead of a headband and always wears a mask of a dragon-like demon and a hooded black cloak. On his left hip he wears chains of chakra-infused silver one of which chains his zanpakuto, Ankokudzura to its sheath. When releasing large amounts of his chakra or in intense situations his sword seems to want to break free from the chain and sheath and starts to slightly emit a black smoke.

History

As a young boy it was clear that GDIX had many talents, especially in genjutsu and sword fighting. Although this was very clear, nobody could really help him develop his skills for GDIX grew up in a poor family with no stimulation to improve himself. Soon after he would attend his first year of ninja-school his father left and his mother quickly remarried another. Not knowing what to do GDIX's self-esteem dropped and soon he was at the bottom of his class and everyone made fun of him.

When the Chunin-exams came close he started noticing his latent talent again, after many lessons in all things ninja his step-father gave him trough the years and the loving care of his mother he noticed how good he was at ninjutsu and genjutsu and soon he started preforming better at missions. But during the Chunin-exams something strange happened. During an attempt to surprise attack an enemy ninja he was placed under a powerful genjutsu. The genjutsu showed him his biggest fear, locked up, unable to chase his desires and filed with despair he blacked out. When he awoke he was shocked that the enemy that attacked him was lying nearly beaten to death by his feet and that a strange black smoke seemed to dissipate before his eyes.

GDIX cleared the exam round and went looking for an explanation for what had happened. He couldn't find anything until he woke up that night feeling a strange energy in his room. Looking around te room he saw nothing out of the ordinary till he caught a glimpse of something in the mirror. When he went over to it and looked in the mirror he saw it, a black demon emitting a black smoke-like energy from his body stood behind him. Looking closer he saw that the demon was not standing behind him, but actually hovered over him clinging to his aura.

Seconds later, GDIX found himself in a dark room, the walls covered with tubes looking like blood-vessels and the demon now standing before him. The demon spoke, "I am …………" "I am the shadow of yourself that you suppressed all these years and now I'm here to take back our life." The demon attacked. The fight however, didn't last long. Although the demon was faster and stronger than GDIX it seemed it had a hard time getting close to GDIX getting caught by the tubes that ran through the room. Soon GDIX also realized that his attacks didn't work on this foe. There was but one thing to do, a sealing jutsu, if GDIX was right this room was the dark corner of himself in which the beast had lived. Trying to seal the beast the tubes like coiling snakes grabbed the beast and chained it of the room leaving it screaming in fury. When the darkness faded GDIX held a sword in his hand and the dark smoke like aura the beast left had turned into a hooded cloak clinging to GDIX's body.

Since then GDIX had changed, he became more solitary, more accurate and above all much stronger.

Not much more can be said about GDIX only some rumors of his abilities float around. And although GDIX seems to have locked the demon and gained some control of it the people know there must be more behind his mask and under his cloak then it seems. But only few know what the true nature and extend is of his power.
