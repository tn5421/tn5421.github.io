Mach Eleven with all stats trained to 11.
Passenger: The White Witch (not included in Totals)
Requires the Village Upgrade Police Lights (+1 Pizza Witch Max Speed) from the Majorkai Sexy Lady Cops
Note that an upgraded Jacket (+Drivetrain) will be crucial in this ride. It is recommended to have at least +200.
If you have Fierce as the Flame (max Gloves) they will give you +5 Drift!
I you are attempting this as RedEye, See The Line will give you (+2) instead, including D-Speed.
The current setup should leave you with a healthy amount of Drivetrain/Durability!
Hopefully the 'Totals' tabs will help you if you wish to customise in your setup (quite likely).

Start

The Spire disappears into the clouds - but you are going to see what is up there! Cici connects a one-time, specially-made OverThruster1 to your Ride. "You'll need this at the end, it'll let you enter OverDrift, if only for a few seconds." Off you go!

Possible Obstacles

Location 1

Danger!
You blast off the top of the Spire, rocketing skywards. The OverDrifter kicks in, and you ride upward, on the air itself!
Speed Needed: 15 or Faster
Drift
Difficulty: 10 _ Successes: 1

If Failed: Autokill!

Location 50

Danger!
As you blast skyward, the world recedes behind you..
Speed Needed: 18 or Faster
Drift
Difficulty: 8 _ Successes: 1

If Failed: Autokill!

Location 80

Danger!
Cici speaks over your Headset - "No matter what, at least one of You has to get up there - this is the only shot we'll ever have.." (why did she emphasize You like that?)
Speed Needed: 24 or Faster
Drive
Difficulty: 9 _ Successes: 3

If Failed: Autokill!

Location 130

Danger!
You look around, and you see a number of other Rides, identically outfitted, all going up as fast as you!
Speed Needed: 21 or Faster
Drive
Difficulty: 10 _ Successes: 1

If Failed: Autokill!

Location 175

Danger!
You crest the Spire, aiming for the heavens, as you realize - none of you will make it! You and another Ride link up, but who will sacrifice themselves? One, two..
Speed Needed: 21 or Faster
Drift
Difficulty: 1 _ Successes: 1

If Failed: Autokill!

Location 200

Danger!
You win! The other Rider nods solemnly. You link your Rides, and they activate their OverThruster! As you approach the barrier, their OverThruster dies out, they unlink, and you activate yours!
Speed Needed: 99 or Faster
Drift
Difficulty: 1 _ Successes: 1
Drive
Difficulty: 1 _ Successes: 1
Destruction
Difficulty: 1 _ Successes: 1

If Failed: Autokill!

Reward

You rocket ahead of the other ride, looking back as you fly upward. As they fall back, the light from the strangely-close sun reflects off of their Ride, and you realize..

The person on the other Ride is you.

The other Ride tumbles towards the ground, and a flash of emerald, digital light erupts from the cockpit, as they - you - Loop out.

You crash through the shifting lattice that is the ceiling of your world, your Ride exploding, and then you fall upwards, through the mists, the only sound a different, more haunting Theme..

You got the Overworld Theme Calling!

Your ascentdescent slows, and you touch down in the middle of a crowded street crossing, in a strange world. The timer on your OverDrifter, the only part of your Ride that survived, shows a constant, blinking..

168 hours.. that's 7 days..

You've completed PizzaWitch!
Congratulations!
