History

  • Early Life

Born into loneliness, Kale Eros began his life wandering the forests in search for a meaning to his life. Traveling for the better part of his childhood, Kale wandered into Slowpoke Village, the village hidden in last place. As he entered the guards stopped him at the gate and asked who he was and for what business he had coming to their village. As he was telling the guards of his situation a Jonin approached. Dael, a young lean built ninja with silver hair worn in a style that resembles a shark's fin, approached the group and inquires about the boy. The guards explain the situation and Dael decided to take Kale with him to the leader of the village, The PokeKage Charlock.

As they walked toward the PokeKage's Residence, Dael probed into the mind of Kale to try and find any useful information about the boys history. Dael had found something very interesting just as they approached the residence, but thought he would ask about it after they had met the PokeKage.

The two entered into the Charlocks study to find the old man reading dirty manga, which he quickly dropped under his desk upon noticing his visitors.

“Oh my, hello, umm, Dael, and….I'm sorry I don't believe we have met.” said the dirty old man.
“PokeKage, this is Kale, a traveling rouge ninja who I have decided to train upon your permission.” asked Dael respectfully. Charlock looked over the boy for a moment and finally asked to speak to the boy in private. Dael bowed instantly and left the room.

“Tell me a little about yourself, Kale, was it? said Charlock.

“Well my name is Kale Eros, and as far back as I can remember I have lived alone. Traveling through the forests of this world I have made my own way following the rules of no man but my own.” stated Kale with determination.

“My my, a very harsh and strong young boy then are we? Well, I have a feeling about you and I will let Dael train you as his apprentice if you will have him, he is one of the strongest and most intelligent Shinobi in the village and if anyone can teach a little rebel like yourself it would be Dael. So, what say you young man?” said Charlock.

Kale stood there for a minute staring into the old mans eyes, trying to see some kind of mis-intent but could only find sincerity and experience. Fighting the urge to run away and be on his own, he weighed his options in his mind and came to the conclusion he would be better suited for living alone after training to become a Shinobi.

“Fine, I will stay and see what the silver shark out there has got to teach me.” said Kale

“Very well, you may stay in one of the empty storage units in the shop district, its not glorious but it all we have for one such as yourself at this moment. Maybe after you train a bit and run some missions you will be able to afford suitable housing. Now then, be off and train hard!” said Charlock waving off the boy as he searched for his lost manga.
