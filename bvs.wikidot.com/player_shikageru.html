Hey, Hey, We're the Ninja! - +20 Stamina
Killer Montage - +50 Stamina

The Flash - Find Legacy-related Special Items in regular missions.
Ascension - If you are a village leader, your village cannot be attacked unless you are bingo'd.

Battle of Champions - Double Friend Points on regular missions
Drunken Fist - Double XP while Sparring
