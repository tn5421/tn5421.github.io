Character Description

He is a well-mannered fighter who has an affinity for awesomeness. He is normally very polite, but when he is angry, he becomes very dangerous and unpredictable. His Bankai unleashes a strange power; he has the ability to control the souls of the dead. He can use those souls to bind things, or even attack enemies.
