Immortal Realm
The slightest thought of this Village
sends you quaking in fear!

+30 Stamina per day, All Rank Type Players may join this Village.

Requirements

  • 0 Village Ryo
  • 0 Resources
  • Village Size: 0

Bonuses

  • +30 stamina per day
  • Upgrades cost 50% less (Ryo and Resources)
  • Kaiju Kage can pay 250 Stamina to create one Basic Resource that the Village produces

Requirement for

  • Drain Amplifier
  • Field Oscillator
  • Impressive History
  • Spire

Comments

  • Only Chunin+, Referral or Survivor Players may join this Village.
    • On April 8, 2010, McMasters lifted the "The StI/Non-Genin rule". So now Survived The Impossible is no longer required to enter an Immortal Realm.
  • This is a free starting upgrade.
  • This upgrade is not optional.
  • The kageship of a village with Immortal Realm can only be given to players of Protagonist rank or above.
  • The kage of a village with Immortal Realm can never leave the village.
  • This upgrade is only given when a player who is a Kaiju (he/she completed The Impossible Mission) starts a new village.
  • This upgrade also affects the village banner, normally a light brown banner with:
<village name> Village
- The Village Hidden In <village place> -
  • Now it will be a red banner with on it:
<village name> Village
The Immortal Realm of the Kaiju <kage name>
- The Village Hidden In <village place> -
