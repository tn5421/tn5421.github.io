Lost Weapons

Location: Beautiful - Passed Over - Touchstone

Team:

  • The messages that you get on the field Essences when you receive the Field Keys Beautiful, Passed Over, Touchstone are hints for the team you must use.
▼ Show Spoiler
▲ Hide Spoiler
  • The Rack
  • Lil' Ro or Lil' Ro Lvl. 2 or Lil' Ro Lvl. 3
  • Olmek

Without the proper Team:

The Field is perfectly black. Everywhere you look, you see reflections of your Teammates..

You can't make anything out in the haze..

With the proper Team:

Eight Symbols float in the nothingness..

Lost Weapon Phase Missing Symbol Acquired Symbol
Tickling Death Bobo - The Machinator
Prancing Haze Stench - The Propagation
Soundless Jade Jet Set - The Terror of Death
Finite Spiral Nine Tails - The Rebirth
Wanton Rose Shoujo - The Temptress of Drama
Voice Enslaver Philosopher - The Prophet
Mottled Wing Tanuki - The Mirage of Deceit
Holy Spur Toroko - The Avenger

1 Lost Weapon:

You got the Field Key Corrupted!

2 Lost Weapons:

You got the Field Key Despaired!

4 Lost Weapons:

You got Lycoris!

6 Lost Weapons:

You got the Field Key Paradise!
Stalkergirl puts away her guns, grabs a giant axe, and walks onto the Field!
Stalkergirl is now Lvl. 3!
She speaks to you. "What comes next is frightening indeed. Take heart - you are never alone."
You've received the Iron Bond!

8 Lost Weapons:

You feel as if you should not be alone.. but no one in your team can withstand this power!
You feel a burst of pure awesome!
You receive the Book of the Twilight and the Crusader's Sword!
Congratulations!

Trivia

  • The symbols in the image are old alchemical symbols for celestial bodies. In the order of the table above, they are: the Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, the Earth, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto.
  • Notably, Venus and Mars are both missing (perhaps for being too well known as gender symbols).
