Konan is a brown haired kunoichi who loves to cause mischief and mayhem.
Loves: Cookies, Chocolate, Cookies, Chocolate, Chocolate, Cookies, Chookies, Cooklate.
Wields: Fusion and Avalon (Sword and Shield)

Konan born from a noble family in Dotaportal village was raised as a princess and was trained in the arts of ninjutsu, genjutsu, taijutsu and nomjutsu. Well versed in shadow mastery and the art of assassination and being top in the academy. That being said, she was much of a tomboy who like to cause mischief and mayhem who really like cookies for some unusual reason.

She entered at the Ninja Academy at the age of 6 and was able to be at the top of her class even after she caused trouble to everyone in the school. At her last year in the academy, she met an unusual ninja in the school grounds doing one handed push-ups… on top of a toothpick. The ninja, who realized Konan’s presence, vanished. “Who the *bleep* was that?” “That was the new Ghost leader… Pain” said Teh Kai our Kage.

At her final test she was told to show all of her strongest abilities and test it out to the village’s strongest ninja… which at that time was none other than Pain. “Show me your true power Princess, because I won’t hold back.” Konan getting irritated launched her most powerful attack which Pain just countered. As they fought and fought she failed to beat him within the time limit. Konan being tired barely muffled the words “I.. Haven’t lost yet. I.. can still.. fight!” The trainer smiled at her and said “This isn’t a battle of who will win or not. This was a test of your abilities if you can fight Pain and reach the time limit. In which you passed.” After that she collapsed and was sent to the hospital.

After Konan’s graduation she promised herself to train hard to be better than Pain. She left the village to train herself and gain new powers. As she ventured on she found herself in a city far from her knowledge. A city filled with oddly dressed people who don’t seem like ninjas at all. Suddenly… A loud screeching noise erupted; it was coming from what seems to be the park. As she reached the park she saw a huge ethereal monster. “What in the world is that?” The monster saw Konan and attacked her. After barely evading that monstrous attack she attacked it and failed to land a hit. “*Huff* *Huff* Why can’t I hit it?!” “It is a Hollow; you can’t damage it with your current ability. You have to have some kind of… special power.” Said a man behind her wearing really green and stripey pants. “You better run before the fashion ninjas kill you.” Said Konan laughing mischievously. “Don’t make fun of the pants. It is the source of ALL power.” The man, after saying that immediately dashed toward the monster and killed it with one blow. “My pants is a pants that will pierce the heavens!” Said the creepy swordsman. “You are strong… and creepy at the same time. But how did you do that? You must tell me!” said a desperate Konan. “I will teach you… after I get some damn nomnoms.”

As Konan followed the warrior she asked “By the way, what's your name?” “…You can call me… uhh… McStripeypants” “…O-kay” said an arrogant Konan.

After walking a long and endless journey of about eleventy-something miles they arrived at their destination… and old shop that looks like it will crumble if the big bad wolf blows air to it. “So we you are supposed to train me in this old shack?” “Nope, we go to where all evil happens…. THE BASEMENT”

As she followed McStripeypants she was led to a ladder going down… WAY down. “Like are we going to trip to hell or something” she said. “Well, yeah, in some sort of way.” Konan as enthusiastic as ever, never showed no sign of fear. “Well you’re a brave one aren’t ‘ya?” “Well if you don’t do anything you don’t get anything” she said. McStripeypants chuckled “Good answer.”

As they reached the training place they immediately began. “Now first I want you to visualize a weapon, a weapon you are familiar with, a weapon that will make give you the power to be… well powerful.” Konan, closing her eyes immediately began visualizing. She saw an image of something and immediately grasped it. McStripeypants felt that image and began chanting something about bunnies and world domination. As he finished chanting they both opened their eyes and saw the… Chicken Leg? “…What the hell? I said weapon!” “Well I suddenly felt hungry” “Well aren’t you feminine.”

They started over again and this time she got it right. What she felt has been forged into a weapon. A weapon that she really wasn’t used to, but hoping to make it one with her soul.
It was a Gladius. “Okay now that you have a weapon. You have to know its power, its soul. You have to fight your new power. Call out its name, only you knows its true name.” Konan focused on her new weapon and thought hard on its name. Suddenly, a voiced spoke in her head. “My name is Fusion, it is not my true name, but its what I have been accustomed to. My power, I will not tell you what it is, you have to fight me to know.” And in a blinding light a large ball of fire filled with feathers and funky music appears. A warrior appeared who looks exactly like her. “You, Why do-“ “No talking. More fighting” said the warrior.

They battled out for over 40 hours already and Konan still doesn’t know what her ability is. “Why don’t you show your power? You’re always copying me!” Konan shouted. “Well use your head a little and you will get the point of my power.” Said the warrior. Konan thought it out and suddenly realized and came to a conclusion. “You… your power. I realized it now. Its mimicry! You copy other peoples abilities!” the warrior smiled “Well that took you a long time.” “Well if weren’t swinging like hell I would’ve had a chance to though it out.” “True, but nevertheless, you showed me that you are worthy of my ability. I grant you my power.” She vanishes in a blinding light and leaves a trail of feathers and her sword form… Fusion.

After Konan obtained her new sword and ability she realizes that there must be something more to it. Three months have passed since she obtained her power and she notices that it is growing every time she uses it. One night, in her sleep she dreamt of a huge battle involving familiar individuals being killed and the one causing all that mess, was none other than her. “It’s not your fault.” Said her dream self. “You couldn’t help yourself from being hungry by power. You wanted power, and you got it, and this is your future.” “Why? How did this happen?” Konan said. “You couldn’t control it… You bit of more than you can chew. You didn’t know how to suppress your abilities.” Konan cried in the face of chaos and destruction. “Only you can prevent this from happening” Said a dying warrior. As Konan approached the man he saw that it was Pain. “You always had that godly gift when you were a child. You always knew what will happen. And only you have the power to prevent this from happening” Said Pain. The images got blurry with Pain leaving her a final message “Never forget your lineage!”

Konan suddenly woke up from a cold sweat and realizes that she should prevent that catastrophe from happening. She ventured out into a long dark forest very far from the city to train herself on how to use her abilities and later know how to suppress it. Days and nights she never gave up but her training seemed effortless. She needed something, something that can tell her what she’s missing. A familiar voice echoed through her head. “…Let it out… The power… You must remember… lineage.” After hearing that she suddenly remembered a flashback. She remembered her 13th birthday “Mommy! What’s this ritual for?” said a young Konan. “It’s your 13th birthday which means you have to go through the Trial of Clairvoyance. Every princess takes it on their 13th birthday.” said Konan’s mother.

Remembering that moment she remembered what she got from that trial and what she can do. “Spirit Walk” a type of trance she can go through to see the future and at that time she also awakened her family bloodline, “Senrigan Tenshi” or the Clairvoyant Angel, which grants her the power to see a little in the future for a price. Although her mother said that hers was different, Konan never knew in what way she was. She uses her power in training to quicken up and prevent more loss in time so she can further deepen her trainings.

Six weeks after her endless training she had a change of power and she was so confident, that the power she obtained from McStripeypants’ training could evolve into something more than what it seems. So she ventured once again to that old shack and hopefully find some help with her training.

As she reached to the old shack, she immediately went inside. As she looked around she notices that nobody was around so she went to the underground area. When she reached the bottom, nobody was there so she just trained by herself until McStripeypants returns. After a few hours she got dizzy and another voice popped in her head.

“Ex Astris, Scientia.
Ex Imperiis, Veritas.
Recipero subitus ita acta est fabula.
Recipero panton quod fabula suscipio.

Abyssus abyssum invocat, vobis es
Nihil sine deus
Nihil sine nefas”

She couldn’t understand what it meant, but somehow she could speak it with no problem at all. “What in the world are you doing here?” as Konan looked behind her it was McStripeypants “Since you are under here, I suppose you want something?” “Yes, I was hoping if you could help me more with my training. Somehow I believe that there is still more than what you taught me” “…So you noticed. Well its earlier than I imagined but yes, there are still more things that you don’t know.” he said. “Come, let us venture further in this endless field.”

After a few minutes of walking they arrived at a place that looks like a gladiatorial arena. “What you are seeking is the power of Bankai, or your second release. What you have right now is your shikai, or your first release. What you need to do right now, is up to you. All I have to do for you is to give you advice and hints on what to do.” He said after going on top of the arena tower. “You may start, ill be here to watch over you, and hopefully not get killed in the process” he said laughing hysterically.

Konan immediately began, but didn’t know where to start, but then she remembered those unfamiliar words and began chanting it;

“Ex Astris, Scientia.
Ex Imperiis, Veritas.
Recipero subitus ita acta est fabula.
Recipero panton quod fabula suscipio.

Abyssus abyssum invocat, vobis es
Nihil sine deus
Nihil sine nefas”

After hearing that McStripey pants jumped over to her side and asked “Where did you learn that? Rather, how can you speak that?” “I don’t know” said Konan “It just echoed through my head and I just began chanting it. I don’t even know what it means” McStripey smirked “So this place really is for you. This is a holy place, a place that can grant peoples desire for power, a power that can be used for good and evil. What you just chanted are words from your spirit sword, calling for you and hoping that you can control its power, and an immeasurable power at that.” Konan being dumbfounded doesn’t know how her spirit sword called out to her since she doesn’t even understand what it means. “From the look of your face, you are waiting for me to translate it out for you right?” Konan smiled “Well that’s what you are here for right?” “Haha, True true.” McStripey pants laughed “Ill translate it for you then;

“From the stars, Knowledge
From Power, Truth
Accept the unexpected and so ends the story
Accept everything and the story begins

Hell invokes hell, for you are
Nothing without god
Nothing without sin”

After hearing what it meant, Konan felt a slight surge of power which grew more and more intense by every second. “What.. what is this?” “You, prove yourself worthy of this power. Control me, and I will be yours. Fail, and you die.” Konan, who fell to her knees trying hard to control every bit of power she felt while enduring immeasurable amounts of pain. After nearly a whole day of enduring it she began to slowly control it little by little and as time passed by she fully controlled everything and obtained her new power, her second release, Bankai, who will give her the form Hrist which gives her the valkyrie like wings and the shield Chaos.
