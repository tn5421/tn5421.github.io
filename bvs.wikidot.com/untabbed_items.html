Items (Untabbed)








Items help to provide an extra bonus to your character's abilities. Most of the items you will receive early in the game are used once as part of a jutsu to increase your chances for success on a mission. As you progress through the ranks of BvS, you will begin to collect permanent items. These items give you a permanent bonus that is active at all times, making permanent items highly valued.
