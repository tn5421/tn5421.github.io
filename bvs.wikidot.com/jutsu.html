Bloodline Jutsu
  • You can learn Jutsu from your allies.
  • The cost to learn the Jutsu is halved if you are hanging out with them.
  • The cost to learn the Jutsu is further halved if you are a WhiteEye and your village has the upgrade Eternal Mirrors
  • Most jutsu have a cost to using them. This can be:
    • Chakra
    • Stamina
    • Items (consumed upon usage)
    • Permanent Items
  • If your chakra is sealed, you may still use jutsu which cost zero chakra
