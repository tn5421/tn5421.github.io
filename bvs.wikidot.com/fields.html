  • The Fields menu is accessed from the village menu. To access it, your village must have a Chaos Gate and you must have the R00t Password.
  • To go to a Field, you must have the required keys. Changing Fields costs 20 Stamina.

Alternate Options:

  • View Other Players (Costs 5 Stamina, shows players recently on the current field)
  • Use Field Oscillator (gives 10 Free Actions, no cost)
