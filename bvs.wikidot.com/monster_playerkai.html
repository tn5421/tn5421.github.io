The <PlayerKai> attacks from beyond the stars!

Monster Information

<PlayerKai> bites you in the eye!
X Stamina lost!
  • HP: 10,000
  • Stat: Gen/Nin/Tai
  • Difficulty: 9

Defeat message

<PlayerKai> returns from whence they came!

Village Upgrade

  • Kaiju Corpse - +10 Daily Stamina

Item

  • Kaiju Memento - +30 Daily Stamina

Trivia

  • This is a generic Kaiju, intended as a template for Kaiju created as a result of completing The Impossible Mission.
  • The image as well as the appear, attack, and defeat messages are all standard for PlayerKai that have not provided unique information for them.
