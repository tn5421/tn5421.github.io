Character Description

Sevejar is a capable Jonin who gives off an aura of capability.

Sevejar stands at an even 6 feet tall; he possesses a slender yet toned build that is reveals more agility than strength. His messy blond hair hangs past his eyes and partially down his face with the tact of a hunting mane. His hair is seemingly kept very clean, something that has more to do with his normal daily hygiene than anything else. His steel grey eyes betray his hostile feelings easily, yet manage to hide his more pleasant ones. With his tall, lean frame and exotic characteristics, Sevejar exemplifies an aristocratic and haughty bearing that belies his years of twenty-two years of age. His chin is spotless of hair, and immaculate, except from times that his duties do not allow him personal time.

Usually, Sevejar is seen in his combat attire, which consists of a long white mantle that came with his entitlement as captain of a Reaper squadron. Peeking at his neck reveals that he bears a Jonin flak jacket underneath the mantle, mainly for the subtle protection it offers from injuries. His flak jacket is composed of lightweight ceramic parts, and its front is emblazoned with the sigil of his Hidden Village. The jacket ends just above his waist. The mantle of the Captain bears the squadron’s symbol on the back. Just under his wrists, master-crafted leather gloves cover his hands and guard them from minor injuries. He wears black pants consisted of wool, and then just underneath his kneecaps appear heavy army boots that embrace his feet entirely. If needs be – social purposes, for example – Firdeth is seen without his flak jacket but a very similar choice of attire nonetheless.

His sword, which Firdeth has dubbed the Darkblood due to its color and strange, continuous dripping of blood from its blade, is a masterly crafted Katana sword. Whether in weight, balance or cutting edge, the Darkblood is without flaw. The sword’s dimensions are: Overall length, 4 feet and 5 inches. The blade length is 3 feet and 5 inches with the rest being the handle’s length. The blade of the sword is bright and immaculate silver steel and on it the sword's name is engraved. The handle itself bears no hand-guard and is crimson in color.

History

Sevejar first obtained his abilities years ago, when he had first ventured into the wrong place in the wrong time. A venture into an old temple of creeping gloom helped him obtain a strange artifact called "the Legacy" by historians, while his meeting (and later saving) Shorty helped him acquire his Reaper bloodline and awake his sword into a terror called Darkblood.

Allies

Annie: A strange, beautiful kunoichi Sevejar met after he was called to proctor a Chuunin exam. She saw that he was quite ruthless in cutting down useless Chuunin wannabes. She walked up to him and asked him if they wanted to "hang out" and Sevejar agreed, thinking that he was going to be into an evening of silly movies and a nice dinner. What he got was an one-night-stand which involved lots of leather, whips and chains. Sevejar hasn't really seen Annie ever since.

Big Bo: Big Bo is a hulk of a man who helped Sevejar crush down a Forest of Evil. And crush he did, with furious blows and mighty stomps, all courtesy of the big man above. He shares the same warmheartedness with his son, Lil' Bo, albeit with more vocabulary tossed in the mix.

Big Shammy: Big Shammy is Lil' Shammy's dad, and Sevejar first found out about that fact when, during training, he managed to hide in his own shadow. As he ventured around hidden he unknowingly startled Big Shammy and made him think that it was his wife that was hunting him down. Despite the man's obvious fear of his wife, he is a good man and, more importantly, a great ninja.

Billy Lvl. 3: Billy and Sevejar go way back, ever since the two were in their academy days. They had first met when Sevejar literally bumped onto Billy while trying to get out of detention. The two stuck together well, augmenting the natural affinity both had for trouble (back when Sevejar still considered himself a kid, at least). The two have gone through many things, including a Crazy Love Triangle between Billy, Pinky and Stalkergirl, as well as trying to master the Sexy Jutsu along with the help of Timmy. Sevejar and Billy learned that the ninja business is serious business when they were fighting an Evil Pirate. Billy used his Legacy powers and defeated the Pirate, deciding to leave his innocence behind when Sevejar was close to dying at the burly outlaw's hands. The two are best friends even now, years of misadventures later. In fact, the pair managed to withstand the Eyepatch of the Soul together, their most difficult adventure so far.

Bruce Jr.: Sevejar was training in blind fighting when he realized that he was not alone. He tossed off the opponent and the two got in a fight that Bruce Jr (who was proved to be the unknown opponent) would have won had he managed to keep silent. Instead, he kept yelling "Feel the power within" and "Go on, you can do it! You're close!", encouraging (more like prompting) Sevejar to beat the boy down. Needless to say, he managed to do it. Again. And again. And once more. The two, strangely, are good sparring partners even now.

Bruce Sr.: Sevejar first met this weird, practically fruity man when he challenged a Dojo master. Hidden amongst the students of this fake Dojo Master, who was secretly training the students into mindless drones of doom and pain, Bruce Sr watched with amazement as a scrawny chuunin beat the Dojo master with a simple hit, causing the previously worshiping students bolt out of the door. The only men in the Dojo being Bruce and Sevejar, the two got into a conversation and actually accomplished a fair share of missions together. Bruce Sr. might seem ridiculous, but it is something that stays only in looks. Sevejar has enjoyed Bruce Sr. antics with K-Dog with a smile on his lips, although he has gotten a headache more often than he'd like to admit…

Bugman: Back when Sevejar was still a genin, he had thought of a bright idea to impress his teacher; he would capture a fly with chopsticks. He did so, but there was a small problem. The fly was Bugman's cousin Elma, and he had broken her wings by mistake. The two did not get much talking done after that, but Sevejar knows that Bugman is a capable shinobi. If only he would get rid of the spiders…

Emosuke: Back in the academy, Sevejar had taken Ninja Leaping Practice as a lesson. Strangely, he had managed to go as far as the best student in the class, who smirked at him. Knowing that the boy was capable, Sevejar approached him and after a lot of pursuing, managed to make him agree on the two passing the Chuunin exam together. Sadly, their paths split after that.
