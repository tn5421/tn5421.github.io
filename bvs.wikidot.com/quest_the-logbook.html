Hypothesis 11 Overview

The Loop is non-virtual.

The energy released on initial activation failed to synchronize with multiple Users, and encompassed a large portion of physical space, causing it to reposition itself to the previous state specified by Loop conditionals. Upon culmination, the Loop repeats this action until actively de-commissioned by the user. The Physical boundary of the Loop extends outward from the projecting mechanism in a lemniscate ("That means infinity symbol", Bugman says helpfully, reading over your shoulder) controlled by a high-energy shell.

Upon complete Loop re-activation, Spiral Signatures are not affected by the system, allowing User, whose signature resonates with the system to 'remember' previous actions. Items attuned to the User also continue to maintain proximity, causing Loops to be non-physical. Pure Signature is also unaffected, causing manifested creations and intelligences lacking physical form that existed at both first and last points in time to maintain memory of previous actions.

Energy required to contain current corruption upon standard deactivation no longer available.

Loop culmination no longer possible. Failsafe option authorized.

"These creatures are what you get from just a small amount of matter being removed from the fabric of energy that runs through reality. Your four friends are now controlled by the same dark animus. If the entire world were to go the same direction.. Bad."

He looks over the anomalies. "This count isn't right. One is missing. Anomaly.. 10."

Research Missing Anomaly >

"It appears that one of the Anomalies is slightly different than the others - this one, here." A picture of Toroko, who you've fought before in the Fields, appears. "There's still a Signature there. Perhaps it has to do with.. yes, the infection occurred against the subject's will. Somewhere inside, it is still fighting against whatever is driving that emptiness. That probably holds true for those four new ones as well."

(new ones? oh, boy.)

A crack reverberates in the back of your mind. "The entire place is coming down. Those things are going to be in here, soon, and based on what I've seen, you have no chance against them - you can't use your current strengths against them, they are immune to the Awesome-based abilities you currently possess. The only record of any attacks that have done anything to affect them are the first Primary User's hand-to-hand abilties.. and the same researcher that was turned."

"I can track down where that particular Anomaly currently is, by filtering out the other ones." Bugman starts analyzing a number of data flows..

Anomaly Overview >

"The Anomalies are:
One: The animus.
Two: The Eleventy-Billion Tailed Fox, controlled by Anomaly One.
Three through Nine: Seven of the Eight Phases, created by the same process as Anomaly Two.
Ten: Toroko.
Eleven: The in-Loop output of the unnamed 'red-haired ninja girl' that became the Fox, and the researcher known as 'the Flash' - you.

"The only chance you have is surviving this onslaught until you Loop, and then using the knowledge you've gained here to somehow get outside the Barrier, get back in where the Failsafe is, and activating it. Doing that will exit the Loop at that point in time, and the two Users, with knowledge of the war's inevitable outcome, may be able to avert it."

"Of course, that will dissolve your self back into those two users."

Bugman pauses, and you see what could be emotion in his normally implacable expression. "Given the alternative, the sacrifice seems worthwhile."

"The plateau has reached its limit. It is about to come down on this entire facility, deactivating this gate, destroying what is left of the facility, and you, if you aren't prepared. There is a path to survival within this Loop. Meet me in the Fields, at the representation of the other side of this world - the Hidden Darkside Holy Ground. I will show you what you need to do to combat these creatures."

- You now have the Field Key Darkside -

Step Into the Gate >

This sends you back into the Fields at Hidden - Darkside - Holy Ground.
