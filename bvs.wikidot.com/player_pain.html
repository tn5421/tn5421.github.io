Character Description

A tall,crimson haired, intelligent man with a dark and painful past.

History

Pain, born in secrecy in the Hidden Village of Death. Pain young at age 5 was trained to become the most Elite ninja of their village. At age 12 he already exceeded the expectations of his masters, even at the age of 15 he trained endlesslly. One day as he trained he felt and dizzy and collapsed, as he was near unconcious he saw a girl running through him and picked him up. When he woke up he saw a girl sleeping in a chair next to him. The girl suddenly woke up as she felt that he has awakened. The girl asked "Are you alright? Whats your name?" Pain still feeling the a little dizzy replied "I have no name, but you may call me as Pain" "Pain huh? Thats a weird name. Im Reika Kaguya a traveler! What happened to you?" she said. "I dont know, this has never happened to me before. Ive had harder training but this is the first time i got tired, this is extremely unusual for me." "Hey were are you from anyway?" Reika asked "The Hidden Village of Death" Pain replied. "Really? Than can I go with you there? Im having a hard time entering your village." Pain still feeling the wobbly as if being hit on the head "Im on training and I wont go back for 2-3 years." "What?! 3 years? Thats awfully long! Where are you going to eat and sleep?" Reika asked. "I hunt and cook my own food, I sleep on treetop branches." Pain said proudly. "Well my you can stay here at my house since im not going anywhere. I was supposedly trying to enter your Village but I cant seem to find it." You're going to let me stay for 3 years?Ive been on this forest for a year now, I cant accept that." Pain said effortlessly "Well I want you to stay, you just collapsed in my own back yard! Im not gonna let that happen again!" Reika said with a smile.

Present Life

3 years have passed and Pain's training is now over. Living in the same house as Reika, Pain fell in love with her. He broke the rules of his village as they were never to Love, never to show Mercy, never to live for something else. As they journeyed to the Hidden Village of Death, Reika asked why is your village so hard to find?" "Weve been hiding ourselves to protect our village from the Hidden Village of Soul, our sworn enemy." "Well atleast you've got me!" Reika said happily. As they reached the village they were swarmed by 13 ninjas and asked them who they were. Pain showed them a scroll and told them "My work is finished and I may now begin"
A ninja that seemed to be their leader asked "Who is this with you, Pain?" Reika trembeled "I-Im Reika Kaguya. Im his lov-" Pain stopped her and said "She helped me while I was acepptance stage of training. She wishes to enter with me and she wishes no harm." "Well then, welcome back Leader!" Pain was appointed as the new leader of the GHOST, Hidden Village of Death's Elite Forces as he has finished his training. "LEADER? So that was what you were training for?" Reika said shockingly. As they entered the village Pain told Reika to have a look around. Pain went to the Kage's Manor. As he arrived and knelt with respect "I have returned Mi'lady from my training." "Good work Pain, as you may have known you are now the Leader and Captain of GHOST our most Elite ninja's and our most prized Guardians. You have my respect as you've trained valiantly and rigourously, and I also heard reports you arrived here with a woman? Is that true?" pain replied "Yes, Mi'lady she has helped with my training and she is a travler who wishes to visit our land." "But that doesnt mean that you love her do you? You are ninja who gains enemies very quickly even if you didnt want one, and you may put her in danger! And to add to that, you do remember your Code right?" the Kage asks "Yes, Mi'lady and I shall live with it." He replied with some regret. "You may take your leave" the Kage said. Pain decided to find Reika and as she found her she was writing something "Where were you?" "I was writing a letter to a friend of mine who said she wanted to see this village." "Oh? But when are you leaving?" "To tell you the truth I wish to stay here for a couple of weeks or months is that alright?" "Yes, as you have already been welcomed in here."

2 years later, after Pain returned after a month long mission he decided to visit Reika. He looked over and over for her but she was nowhere to be found. After hours and hours of searching he heard and explosion in the direction of the Kage's manor, he decided to go to the manor. As he reached the manor there were GHOST members dead and some clinging to life, Pain asked one of them "Who did this?" the ninja replied "Hidden *cough* Soul… Mi'lady.. danger.." Pain understood every his comrade's dying words. As he reached the Kage's room he was too late as she is already Murdered as if she never knew they're being attacked. As he went to the Kage's body he noticed a cloaked figure. "YOU! YOU DID THIS!" as the cloaked ninja turned his back who reavealed to bo Reika, "REIKA?! Why?!" Pain said in anger. "My name is Reika Kaguya Sannin of the Hidden Village of Soul, Ive been planning to annihilate everyone on this village. Why? My parents were killed by this village and your GHOST's! Thats why!" Pain holding A kunai that Reika made for him, "So this was just an act?" "I knew who you are, I was the one who threw a dart at you to make you uncounsious, you wont stop me Pain, I know all of your fighting technicques!" Reika said. "Well then ive learned a new technicque that not even you know about, sadly youre the only one who will see it, Prepare to die Reika! My love." As they fought Pain remembered every single moment they shared together, the laughter, love, and joy they shared together which only fueled his hatred more and more. As Reika fell in the floor, she screamed "Why would do this to me? I thought you loved me?!" Pain, as blood and betrayal covered his face smirked and said calmly "I feel no love… I show no mercy… I live for nothing else." Pain killed Reika and held her to his heart.

Bearing the sight of his village's demise, the pain of betrayal of his loved one, the death of his comrades, and the fate of his master all weighed inside of him. He promised to live by his code of honor, relinquishing his title as Leader of Ghost, He leaves his past behind and wishes to join a village a village protecting it and living in it by his code.

2 Years have passed and he has found a worthy village to fight for until his dying day
He now joins the Dotaportal Village with the help of their kage Teh Kai and their loyal ninjas to find his true calling and hopefully find a friend he knew but might have been forgotten.

As Pain gained ranks in Dotaportal Village he took special test that he never knew existed

Path of the Reaper

As Pain did missions to help his newfound village he obtained somewhat ominous items and that tempts him to keep. As he continued to collect those items he met a figure whom he never saw before, he met Shorty and found out that she was on a mission and decided to help her as he saw that her enemy is far more stronger than what he encountered before. As they defeated the horrendous monster, Shorty sensed an enormous hidden power in Pain and told him about the Reapers. Pain gaining insight of a new way to fight decided to undergo the training Shorty explained to him.

As he reached the Reapers haven Shorty decided to let him go on his own for now. As he wandered in a village he never knew existed he was attacked by a ninja like warrior, "Halt! You are tresspassing in a forbidden territory! You are a human, you are forbidden in this land." Pain kept in silence as he always have and drew his sword. As he fought the warrior he noticed unfamiliar powers that surprised him, near the end of the battle Pain's sword broke, even though he thought he will be defeated he fought the warrior with his exceptional skills in Taijutsu. Pain defeated the warrior with his own hands, the warrior shocked of his potential asked him what his purpose was. The warrior identifies himself as Tats, he told him of a man that could help him train to become a reaper, Tats told Pain to return to earth and find a certain shop.

As pain returned he was directly thrown in to the front of a shop, Pain thought that that maybe the shop the reaper told him about. As he went inside he met the owner and asked him if he new Tats, as Pain asked him he was disturbed by the mans funny looking stripey pants. Pain told him, "You stripeypants! I was told you could teach me the ways of the reaper." "How did you know my name?" for the first time in the world Pain shouted in vain "Jesus Christ your name is Stripeypants?"

As their introductions came to a close, MC Stripeypants taught him to unleash his power of the reaper. He trained endlessly until he obtained a power he never knew he had.

As of that moment, Pain obtained his shikai and his most prized sword, Lumina.

Bankai

After witnessing the newfound power Pain obtained, MC Stripeypants felt that Pain can obtaine a higher level than what he just learnt.

MC Stripeypants told Pain to train for 3 days in his basement. As MC Stripeypants returned after only 16 hours to check up on Pain, he saw pain waiting for him and told him that a voice called out to him. MC Stripeypants shocked at the early advancement told to him to reach out to the voice and continue training. After endless battles with different helpful reapers, Pain called out to a new form of his Power. "Illuminate, Lumina!" as MC Stripeypants smirked at his new student's achievement. Pain caught MC Stripeypants' attention as he threw his sword in the sky and shouted, "Lumina et Aether!", MC Stripeypants was shocked at the power Pain's sword granted.

Pain obtained his Bankai, Lumina et Aether, the light of the heavens.

Continuing his Journey

After obtaining his Bankai, Pain continued to his journey and finally decided to find his long lost friend, Konan.

Pain, before he started his training as the GHOST leader he met Konan, who was still a childish and self-centered brat who tried to hit him.

As years passed Konan and Pain grew closer and became close friends, in fact they are the sannins of the Village of Death. Pain felt guilty of leaving Konan behind, as he killed Reika he went to look for Konan in the burning Death Village. He searched everywhere he could, no place left unchecked. He thought that Konan might have died in the attack and felt more guilty and fled in despair.

Years and years have passed and Pain still isnt comfortable of the fact that Konan might still be Alive. He secretly does sidetrips on every allied village's missions to find any information regarding Konan. He knew for the fact that Konan never has been injured due to her bloodline. The Minato Clan's and their bloodline, "God's Eye" the most respected and feared eye technique in their village, and the Minato Clans most prized daughter, the most talented of all of the new generation of trainees, Konan. Pain knowing full well of what Konan is capable of kept believing that she is still alive somewhere.

One day Pain accepted a mission for one of Dotaportals allied villages, the TFFA village, Pain he heard of a rumor of an undeafeated warrior who came to their village in seek of information about a man she was acquainted with. Pain even knowing that it may or may not be Konan tried to look for her in the village. Hours have passed and he has not found any trace of her, so he decied to continue on the mission. On his way back from a succesfull mission Pain wore a his mask to hide his identity to the civilians of the village for their own safety. As he nearly reached the gate he was attacked by a cloaked warrior who fights with a familiar style. Pain with exceptional knowledge in battle and his accurate strategies grabbed the warrior fist and asked who he was. As the warrior removed his cloak, Pain shed a tear, a tear that never existed even after the death if his loved one. He knew the instant he saw that long blue hair and that funny looking hairpiece that it was in fact, Konan.

Pain knelt and held her hand and gave her a black rose, the GHOST's symbol, as she reached for her face he slowly removed his mask and said "Would you forgive a man who left without saying a word?" Konan, looking at him also crying said "I could say the same thing you know." "I thought you died. I looked everywhere even after the village was destroyed!" Pain shouted. "I was teleported to the Light village… and I destroyed it. I dont know what happened even until now." Pain hugged her tight and said "Im glad I could see that havoc-causing face of yours one last time." "I never thought id be this pissed to see that mask again." Pain said in a faint voice, "You are the only one I have left, and im not going to lose you again." Konan smiled and replied with a failed attempt to hit him on the head and said "Now ive got a chance to beat you once and for all, and pay you back that 30,000 ryo I owe you at the Jonin party." They both laughed and went back to the village together. Pain knew that Konan also achieved bankai and has obtained her sword "Fusion". Together they did missions even though they were from different villages, and together they finished everysingle mission inculding the the discovery of the forbidden land, The Wasteland.

End of Season 1. Probably

Techniques and Jutsu

Illuminate, Lumina!

Shikai

Lumina takes the form of a kotetsu which emits light waves forming various forms

Shear of Light - A semi-circular wave that slices anything in its path.
Spear of Destiny - A spear shaped wave that strikes anything with an immeasurable amount of force.
Hand of God - Causes lumina to take the form of a Gauntlet which ables him to do godspeed attacks.
Cloak of the Heavens- A wave which shrouds Pain in light causing him to move fast with the speed of light.

Bankai

In bankai Lumina changes to Lumina et Aether(Light of the Heavens)

Lumina changes in to a dual double edged sword which emits a powerful light that connects the power of the heavens to Pain. Lumina enshrouds pain in a light which forms a Cape and a Halo which give him an insight of what an enemy is capable of and give him the ability to cast various spells.The light also binds the soul of his enemies and brings them to a Labyrinth of Light.Pain gains sight of his enemies and his foes are allowed to see him so that he can fight bring forth justice. Pain attacks with near dead accuracy with light speed attacks.

No enemy that has entered the Labyrinth has ever escaped alive.

Souls that have been defeated in the Labyrinth shall be forever be trapped in an neverending sea of light.
