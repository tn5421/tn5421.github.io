Getting Our Own Village Page

Creating your own Wiki page for your village is easy! Just type your village name into the box below and hit "Create My Page!" You'll be delivered to your own custom page which you can edit with your village's information. Only you (and the administrators) can edit this page, unlike the rest of this Wiki, so you don't have to worry about someone slandering your good name! You do need a full site membership to create your village page however, but just register at Wikidot account. Most requests for site membership are handled within a few hours. We are currently requiring site membership for this to try and stem the possibility of vandalism.

Please only create a Village page if you are the leader of your village! If you would like your Village to have a page and you are not the leader, please speak with your leader!
