A Ninja-Mas Robbery

The HoClaus needs your help!

(Requirements: December 13th-25th)

Notes

Prologue

As you stroll down a Village Path on an errand, you notice a twinkle in the sky. Your curiosity, however, quickly turns to panic as a vehicle hurtles down towards you! You dive into the bushes as the HoClaus loudly crashes to the ground where you once stood.

You emerge, going to check on him, and he pops out of the wreckage, free of injury, brushing himself off.

"There's been a robbery! Help me out, and together we can save Ninja-Mas!"

Grabbing your arm, he puts his finger to the side of his nose, and in a sparkle of Ninja-Mas magic, transports you both away!

I was just going to the store for a snack, why does everyone come to me with their problems >

Part 1

A Strange Photo
Helpers run around, using giant swaths of holiday tape to patch holes in the HoClaus' magic factory. Giant holiday machinery sputters and coughs, shooting cookies and wrapped presents all over the place.

"A whole bunch of machinery and presents got swiped! While we work on fixing this place up, can you find the culprits? All we have to go on is this picture.."

Wait a second.. >

You look at the grainy image of a dozen figures dashing away from the factory with bags of goodies and carts of materials. While the HoClaus points out info, you feel that something seems off about the robbers..

is it the sunglasses? "They must be hiding their faces!"

How some have little clawy arms, and others are big and stompy? "What a diverse group of thieves! They must have come from all over to plan this?"

The.. tails? "That must be them dragging stuffs behind them!"

Did the HoClaus get robbed by.. dinosaurs?

Continue A Ninja-Mas Robbery >

Epilogue

"What? No. Dinosaurs? Come on, that's absurd."

A candy cane explosion startles the both of you. "Oh, man, I gotta get back to work here. Check around the different parts of the world, and see if you can't find any suspicious individuals. Really look under things, see what you can find. In fact, I bet you can find one per day. Once you find all twelve, come back here. Okay - go and save Ninja-Mas!"

(to help you get started, maybe check the Consumables page, to see how this works!)

Check the Consumables page

sneak sneak sneak >_>

You notice a sunglasses-clad cool dude walking by carrying a huge armful of loot. You yell at them, and they drop it with a clatter, scampering away!

You have recovered Twelve Wicked Skateboards! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Jutsu page (on day 2)

yikes o_o

You adjust your headband in a mirror, and the reflection doesn't seem right - it isn't a mirror, it's a hole in a fence, and one of the thieves is on the other side! They take off running, leaving behind a pile of stuffs.

You have recovered Eleven Radical Headbands! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Spar page (on day 3)

zzzz -_-

That sign of someone advertising ramen is not just a sign! When your eyes meet with the figure on it, they burst out of the sign and fly(?) away.

You have recovered Ten Giant Phones! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Team page (on day 4)

oh nooo T_T

The person marking down teams at the counter matches someone in the photo! When you get to the front of the line, they see who you are and run out the door, trailing stolen presents!

You have recovered Nine Leg Warmers! (huh, an odd number. weird) The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Summons page (on day 5)

YIKES O_O

As you wait at a crosswalk, the lid of the trash can next to you slooooowly rises, and you make eye contact with the one inside. They eep, and run down the street, leaving behind a bin of stuffs!

You have recovered Eight Hacky Sacks! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Bucket page (on day 6)

hey dont look over here >_>

Amongst a row of boxes, you notice that one of them has feet! When you touch it, it flies off, and the culprit under it thunders off, leaving a trail of loot.

You have recovered Seven Shutter Shades! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the WorldKaiju page (on day 7)

look somewhere else please <_<

Some guy jogging past you is walking his dog at the same time, that's pretty cool.. wait a minute, that is one of the robbbers! And, so is the dog! You yell, and they take off in different directions, emptying their pockets. (Fake dogs have pockets, it seems?)

You have recovered Six Pairs of Jellies! (they are shoes, kinda) The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Number One page (on day 8)

aiya >_<

The HoClaus waves at you with his tail from his sleigh.. wait a second, that's not the HoClaus! You give chase, and in their haste they crash into a billboard, running off with what they can from it!

You have recovered Five Neon Socks! (one is missing..) The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Village page (on day 9)

nothing to see here =_=

A random reporter interviews you for your opinion on ramen flavors. A few minutes in, you realize that A, the microphone is just a prop, and B, this is one of those culprits! You chase them but they get away, trailing stuffs in their wake.

You have recovered Four Walkmans! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Arena page (on day 10)

yeep ._.

You take the shuttle to the Arena, and the driver looks a little familiar to you.. hey, it's a thief! You both wait for him to pull up to the last stop (safety first!) and then mayhem ensues as you chase and wrestle through a fullly-packed vehicle, before he bursts out the back and escapes!

You have recovered Three Mixtapes! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Trophies page (on day 11)

no one here just us robots ]_]

There's a dancing robot display outside of a noodle shop. That's odd, why would use advertise noodles with robots? You peer at the robot, and it visibly begins to sweat. The moment you move toward it, the thief inside runs away, bits of bad robot costume trailing in its wake.

You have recovered Two Boom Boxes! The HoClaus pops in and takes them back to the factory.

Look for someone else, somewhere else, tomorrow!

Check the Consumables page again (on day 12)

oh hi ^_^

..and a Secret Dino Par-ty~

Back where you started, it's that same thief, just standing there next to a sign that says "You already caught this one, don't worry about it"! You catch him in a huge tackle, rolling down a hill, and end up at the feet of.. the HoClaus? He motions for both of you to follow him! (Why is this twelve days, not eleven, anyway?)

Go to the Quest Menu!

Notes

You only have to start this quest, and possibly find the first thief, during the time period specified. For example, if you start this quest and do day 1 on December 15, so that day 12 comes on December 26, that works just fine. Further delays also work. And if you want to start when it is not December, there are the holiday themes.
